============================================================
ADVISOR REVIEW RETEST
============================================================

Before:
Coverage Orchestrator advisor output was generic:
- "Forge should stop and request review before giving a stronger answer."

After:
Event Advisor Review provides:
- advisorSummary
- whatToAskNext
- whatNotToPromise
- evidenceToRequest
- expectationRisk

Example:
Hospitalized in Houston

Advisor output:
- Summary: This situation should be handled as HOSPITALIZATION, FOREIGN_EVENT.
- Ask next: Did this happen in Mexico or another country?
- Do not promise coverage.
- Request diagnosis and country.
- Expectation risk: Client may assume national coverage works the same abroad.

Result:
PASS. Advisor guidance is now actionable.

============================================================
CLIENT REVIEW RETEST
============================================================

Before:
Client Review was document-based, not event-aware.

After:
Event Client Review provides:
- whatWeKnow
- whatWeNeed
- whyWeCannotAnswerYet
- nextStep

Example:
Childbirth abroad

Client output:
- We know this may involve care outside Mexico.
- We still need country and delivery date.
- A reliable answer needs medical facts and policy documents before anyone should promise an outcome.
- Next step: Did this happen in Mexico or another country?

Result:
PASS. Client explanation is understandable and non-technical.
