# Forge GMM Event Evidence Packets

Status: PRE-IMPLEMENTATION / FINAL PRE-ENGINE DISCOVERY

## Purpose

Define event-specific evidence packets for GMM Coverage Intelligence.

## Accident Packet

Required:

- Accident date.
- Cause of injury.
- First attention or first expense date.
- Territory.
- Insured identity.
- Product/caratula.

Recommended:

- Medical diagnosis.
- Accident report or hospital record.
- Activity context.

Optional:

- Photos, advisor summary, witness context.

Human Review Evidence:

- Alcohol/drug evidence.
- Intentional conduct evidence.
- Racing/professional sport evidence.
- Conflicting date evidence.

## Illness Packet

Required:

- Diagnosis or medically clear condition.
- First symptom date.
- Diagnosis date.
- First expense date.
- Policy start/continuity.
- Territory.

Recommended:

- Clinical file.
- Diagnostic studies.
- Treating physician report.

Optional:

- Client symptom summary.

Human Review Evidence:

- Prior medical records.
- Prior expenses.
- Pre-policy symptoms/treatment evidence.

## Maternity Packet

Required:

- Mother insured status.
- Mother age.
- Coverage continuity.
- Expected birth date or birth date.
- Territory.
- Assisted reproduction status.
- Caratula maternity benefit and deductible type.

Recommended:

- Clinical history.
- Ultrasound.
- Birth certificate after birth.

Optional:

- Prenatal care summary.

Human Review Evidence:

- Assisted reproduction documents.
- First-trimester notice.
- Coverage continuity proof.
- Deductible en Exceso confirmation.

## Pregnancy Complication Packet

Required:

- Complication diagnosis.
- Mother insured status.
- Coverage continuity.
- Event date.
- Territory.

Recommended:

- Hospital record.
- Specialist report.
- Clinical file.

Optional:

- Prenatal history.

Human Review Evidence:

- Voluntary/punishable abortion facts.
- Assisted reproduction notice.
- Conflict between complication and birth benefit.

## Newborn Packet

Required:

- Newborn identity.
- Mother insured status.
- Biological/gestational relationship.
- Whether maternity was covered.
- Birth date.
- Inclusion request date.

Recommended:

- Birth certificate.
- Hospital newborn record.
- Policy inclusion request.

Optional:

- Pediatric notes.

Human Review Evidence:

- Late inclusion evidence.
- Medical selection documents.
- Congenital/genetic diagnosis timing.

## Foreign Care Packet

Required:

- Country/location.
- Date of care.
- Urgent vs programmed status.
- Optional foreign coverage in caratula.
- Foreign stay duration.

Recommended:

- Foreign medical records.
- Proof of residence/stay.
- Insurer programming/notice evidence.

Optional:

- Travel itinerary.

Human Review Evidence:

- Endorsement.
- Access-to-medical-information consent.
- Programmed/non-programmed conflict evidence.

## Catastrophic Disease Abroad Packet

Required:

- Diagnosis category.
- Optional catastrophic foreign coverage.
- Country/location.
- Programmed status.
- Date of treatment.

Recommended:

- Specialist diagnosis.
- Clinical studies.
- Treatment plan.

Optional:

- Foreign provider estimate.

Human Review Evidence:

- Disease classification dispute.
- Cancer staging/type.
- Transplant donor/compatibility records.

## Dental Packet

Required:

- Dental issue type.
- Accident vs disease context.
- First attention date if accident.
- Provider/network status.

Recommended:

- Dental specialist report.
- X-rays/studies.
- Prior authorization for accident dental.

Optional:

- Treatment estimate.

Human Review Evidence:

- Direct/immediate accident causality.
- Precious-metal/prosthesis component evidence.

## Medication Packet

Required:

- Medication name.
- Diagnosis/covered claim relationship.
- Prescription.
- Pharmacy invoice.
- Pharmacy path.

Recommended:

- COFEPRIS/permit evidence.
- Treating physician note.

Optional:

- Pharmacy list lookup.

Human Review Evidence:

- Medication tied to excluded diagnosis.
- Orphan drug/gene therapy classification.
- Permit ambiguity.

## High-Specialty Treatment Packet

Required:

- Treatment/procedure name.
- Diagnosis.
- Medical necessity.
- Prior authorization status.
- Second valuation status if required.
- Provider certification if required.

Recommended:

- Clinical file.
- Specialist report.
- Diagnostic studies.
- COFEPRIS approval where relevant.

Optional:

- Treatment protocol.

Human Review Evidence:

- Experimental treatment question.
- Equal/superior alternative treatment.
- Missing authorization.
- Provider certification ambiguity.

## Hospitalization / Surgery Packet

Required:

- Diagnosis/procedure.
- Hospital.
- Hospitalization/surgery date.
- Urgency status.
- Product/caratula.
- Territory.

Recommended:

- Hospital record.
- Surgery report.
- Provider agreement status.
- Hospital level.

Optional:

- Provider estimate.

Human Review Evidence:

- Hospital-level mismatch.
- Multiple procedures.
- High-specialty overlap.

## Assistance Service Packet

Required:

- Assistance service requested.
- Location.
- Distance from residence if relevant.
- Channel used.
- Event reason.

Recommended:

- Assistance case number.
- Provider coordination record.

Optional:

- Travel/hotel documentation.

Human Review Evidence:

- Service not coordinated through required assistance channel.
- Inaccessible/unsafe location facts.
