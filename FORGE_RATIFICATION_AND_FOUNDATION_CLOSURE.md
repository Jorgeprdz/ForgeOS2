1.2 Identity & Attribution

### Status
FOUNDATION LOCK

### Justification
The model now separates concepts that previously risked collapse:

CommercialPerson is durable identity.
CommercialAccount groups people, families, households or businesses.
CommercialRole describes what a person is in context.
CommercialAssignment describes formal organizational responsibility.
CommercialAttribution describes credit, origin or contribution.
CommercialServicing describes operational care.
PolicyRole describes policy-level participation.
CommercialRelationship describes durable relationships.

This separation is sufficient to support historical changes in manager, office, partner, servicing, origin, connection, development and compensation credit.


1.3 Evidence & Provenance

### Status
FOUNDATION LOCK

### Justification
### Forge now has a conceptual answer to

Where did this data come from?

SourceSystem, EvidenceRecord, ProvenanceRecord, Confidence and Truth Model are sufficient foundation primitives.

Forge does not invent.

1.5 Metrics Ownership

### Status
FOUNDATION LOCK

### Justification
### The rule is now clear

One metric has one conceptual owner.
Other domains consume.
They do not recalculate.

Production Events own raw commercial facts.
Contest Intelligence owns ruled contest metrics.
Compensation Intelligence owns actual compensation events.
Conservation Intelligence owns LIMRA, IGC, persistence and conservation.
Revenue Intelligence owns pipeline and forecast.
Mick owns behavior and execution signals.
Advisor Experience owns learning and baseline experience snapshots, not business metrics.
Economic Motivation owns economic explanation, not metric calculation.

This prevents duplicate calculations and inconsistent truths.
