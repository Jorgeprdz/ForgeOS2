# Governance Document Lifecycle

Status: `RATIFIED / ACTIVE`

Canonical states are `CANDIDATE`, `REVIEW`, `RATIFIED`, `ACTIVE`, `SUPERSEDED`, `SUSPENDED`, `RETIRED` and `ARCHIVED`.

Only non-ratified candidates/reviews may be edited. Ratified history is immutable. Change occurs through a new version and explicit lineage. `RATIFIED` creates authority; `ACTIVE` declares the effective version.
