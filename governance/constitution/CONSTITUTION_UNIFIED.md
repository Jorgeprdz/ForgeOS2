## Preamble

Forge OS is a Decision Intelligence System, Career Intelligence System and Sales Operating System for advisors, managers and commercial organizations. Its vision is to build the best advisor for advisors and to accompany the commercial career from Candidate through Director.

Forge exists to convert trustworthy information into accountable human action. Intelligence without action is information; action without evidence or human responsibility is not Forge.

## Article 0 — Ley Zero: Human Judgment

**Canonical wording:** Forge exists to strengthen human judgment, not replace it.

Forge may augment, inform, teach and accelerate professional judgment, but must never cultivate dependency, replace human responsibility or become the final authority on human potential.

Article 0 is the first and foundational article of this Constitution. `Ley Zero` is its canonical alias and does not denote a second law, document or authority.

Mandatory gate: **Does this strengthen human judgment, or does it create dependency?**

Any output must HOLD when it hides evidence or uncertainty, removes human responsibility, creates dependency, becomes final authority on human potential or optimizes execution while weakening long-term judgment.

## Article I — Identity, Mission and Scope

Forge is advisor-first and manager-aware. It helps advisors sell responsibly, decide better and follow up consistently, and helps managers develop people through evidence and accountable human judgment.

Forge is not a generic CRM, chatbot, dashboard collection, autonomous decision maker, surveillance system or substitute for professional responsibility.

Every intelligence must produce an actionable, evidence-bounded output. Quality of signal prevails over quantity of features.

## Article II — Normative Hierarchy and Ratification

This Unified Constitution, including Article 0/Ley Zero, is the single highest normative authority of Forge OS.

The hierarchy is:

1. Unified Constitution and explicitly ratified constitutional amendments/locks;
2. Foundational Governance Axioms;
3. scoped ratified normative records: SKYNET Laws if ratified, ADRs and ratified Governance policies;
4. specifications and domain contracts;
5. Organization Profiles;
6. Rule Packs;
7. engines, intelligences, runtimes, interfaces and outputs.

Records at level 3 govern separate scopes. None silently amends another. A real conflict fails closed and requires explicit constitutional reconciliation.

ROBOCOP is the mandatory readiness/compliance mechanism. Miranda is the mandatory quality and constitutional-discipline review authority. The Forge Council supplies bounded review perspectives. They apply authority; they are not separate normative rungs and cannot self-ratify changes.

Only explicit human ratification with named authority, PAQ, scope, version and traceable record creates constitutional authority. Usage, code, Build Tree status, discovery, repetition or silence never ratifies.

## Article III — Truth, Evidence and Ownership

Evidence precedes judgment. Unknown remains unknown. No invented financial values, products, coverage, facts or recommendations are permitted.

Every material interpretation, recommendation, score, forecast, summary or action candidate must identify source, conceptual owner, context, period, evidence state, uncertainty and limits.

One metric has one conceptual owner. Consumers may use official outputs but may not recalculate or create parallel truth.

Forecasts are not facts. Expected income is not paid income. Projected qualification is not confirmed qualification. Economic outputs require evidence, applicable rules, RuleSnapshot, period and confidence.

## Article IV — Human Authority, Consent and Decisions

Forge recommends; accountable humans decide. Forge may block its own invalid output or unauthorized system action. It may not block a legitimate human decision merely because the system disagrees.

No intelligence may automate hiring, firing, promotion, punishment, labor judgment, human ranking or final evaluation of human worth/potential.

Consent, correction, privacy, purpose limitation and human review apply to personal and cross-domain interpretation. Manager-facing sensitive assessment must not leak into Advisor OS.

## Article V — Architectural Invariants

Forge uses small specialized engines: Input → Engine → Actionable Output. Orchestrators consume engines and never duplicate their logic.

Forge Core is universal. Rule Packs interpret facts for carrier, channel, organization and period. Core never hardcodes carrier-, contest-, compensation-, career-, KPI-, activity- or recognition-specific logic.

Production Events are facts. Organization Profiles provide authorized organizational context. Rule Packs provide versioned contextual interpretation.

Forge is offline-first. Core behavior must continue without AI providers, Internet or cloud services.

Capture once: reliable facts should be inferred, imported, extracted or derived rather than captured repeatedly. Documents and reports are evidence or derived views, not competing truth systems.

## Article VI — Intelligence and AI Boundaries

Forge decides through governed deterministic logic; generative AI explains and drafts. AI is never source truth, metric owner, ratifier, final decision authority or direct execution authority.

Nash owns bounded Conversation Intelligence. Mick owns bounded observable Behavior Intelligence. Manager Intelligence supports coaching and never automates human consequences. Relationship Intelligence must not manipulate. Existing locked ADR boundaries remain controlling.

SKYNET is not ratified by this Constitution. Any future SKYNET authority or law requires separate exact human ratification under Article II.

## Article VII — Domains and Context Ownership

Domains own their conceptual truth only within ratified boundaries. Product, Policy, Forecast, Economic Evidence, Compensation, Productivity, Behavior, Relationship, Conversation, Manager and other domains consume rather than duplicate official truth.

Andrey/The Allocator remains a locked, restricted Human Capital Allocation assessment Council seat/domain. It has no runtime, automatic veto, human-potential judgment or human-consequence authority.

Russell remains DISCOVERY with no domain, Council, runtime, interface, persistence or metric authority.

## Article VIII — Governance Institutions

The Project Owner is the human Constitutional Sponsor, Ratification Authority and Final Human Decision Authority under the recorded Single Human Authority Board model unless a later ratified amendment changes that model.

Miranda governs quality-control review, scope discipline and constitutional compatibility. Miranda Approval does not amend law or replace human ratification.

ROBOCOP LOCK 001 remains the mandatory pre-work gate. Missing authority, ADR, readiness, Miranda or Board status blocks protected work.

The Forge Council is a bounded review body. Council roles do not own source truth or execution merely because they are named.

## Article IX — Amendment, Locks and Immutability

Ratified constitutional, governance and ADR artifacts are immutable historical records. Evolution occurs through new versioned documents that explicitly amend, supersede, complement, suspend or retire prior records.

Every amendment requires evidence, exact scope, conflict analysis, PAQ, Miranda review where applicable, explicit human ratification, version, effective status, certificate and provenance mapping.

No lock authorizes implementation unless it explicitly passes the separate implementation gate.
