5. REGISTRY PHILOSOPHY

- Every truth claim needs an owner.
- Every metric has one owner.
- Consumer domains may consume truth, not redefine it.
- Presentation domains explain truth, not rewrite it.
- Forecast owns scenarios, not facts.
- Business Planning owns action paths, not source truth.
- Manager Intelligence owns coaching context, not enforcement.
- Advisor Experience owns explanation/help, not truth.
- Economic Motivation owns economic presentation posture, not economic truth.
- Unknown owner means strong output must be blocked.
- Ownership does not equal validity; ownership only identifies who may validate or interpret.

6. CORE QUESTIONS THE REGISTRY MUST ANSWER

For every source / claim / output, the registry must answer:
- What is the source type?
- What is the evidence type?
- What claim is being made?
- Which domain owns this truth?
- Which ADR governs the ownership?
- Which domains may consume it?
- Which domains may not reinterpret it?
- What state fields are required before use?
- Does this claim require source validation?
- Does this claim require human review?
- Is the owner known, unknown or conflicting?
- What output strength is allowed?
- What output must be blocked?

8. OWNERSHIP STATUS VOCABULARY

- owner_confirmed: The conceptual owner is unambiguously identified and available.
- owner_candidate: A potential owner is identified but requires further contextual evidence.
- owner_unknown: No clear owner can be determined; strong claims blocked.
- owner_conflicting: Multiple domains claim ownership; requires resolution or ADR review.
- owner_not_applicable: The item does not represent a truth claim or metric.
- consumer_allowed: The consuming domain is authorized to read the truth claim.
- consumer_restricted: The consuming domain is blocked from accessing the truth claim.
- reinterpretation_blocked: A domain attempts to alter the semantic meaning of the owned truth.
- human_review_required: The claim touches sensitive boundaries and cannot be automatically asserted.
- validation_required: The source evidence needs explicit validation before the owner can process it.

10. DOMAIN OWNERSHIP MAP

A. Evidence Ownership / Source Validity
OWNER OF: Provenance, validity states, document authenticity, raw system-observed activity logs, raw quotes/illustrations.
NOT OWNER OF: Business meaning of the document, behavior patterns.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: All core intelligence engines.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot alter business truth.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-001

B. Metric Truth
OWNER OF: Canonical numerical outputs and calculations.
NOT OWNER OF: Forecasts, managerial actions.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Business Planning, Manager Intelligence, Compensation Intelligence.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot be redefined by UI or Business Planning.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-002

C. Product Truth
OWNER OF: Carrier product rules, coverages, limits.
NOT OWNER OF: UDI source truth, FX source truth, Forecasts, compensation values.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Forecast Truth, Policy Truth, Advisor Experience.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot treat quote rules as fact unless supported by documentation. May consume UDI/FX only when documentation requires currency semantics.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-005

D. Policy Truth
OWNER OF: Issued policy states, active coverage, confirmed cancellation events, paid premiums (with payment evidence).
NOT OWNER OF: General product rules, anticipated future premiums, quote interpretations.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Compensation Intelligence, Relationship Intelligence, Economic Motivation.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot treat quote as policy. Cannot treat issued premium as paid premium.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-006

E. Forecast Truth
OWNER OF: Future scenarios, projections, cancellation risk scenarios, projected UDI/FX using explicit assumptions.
NOT OWNER OF: Confirmed cancellation events, current facts, issued policy truth, product rules, raw quotes.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Business Planning, Economic Motivation, Advisor Experience.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Forecasts cannot be presented as guaranteed facts. Cannot invent payment.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-007

F. Economic Evidence
OWNER OF: Validity of economic values (monetary, premiums, paid, issued, quoted, sums insured, UDI values, FX rates, currency conversion sources, economic assumptions).
NOT OWNER OF: Compensation interpretation, product rules, policy events, motivation posture.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Economic Motivation, Relationship Intelligence, Forecast, Product Truth (for semantics).
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot invent values or redefine compensation logic.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-008

G. NBA (Next Best Action)
OWNER OF: Prioritization of recommended actions.
NOT OWNER OF: The decision itself.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Advisor Experience, NASH.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot enforce actions as mandatory decisions.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-003, ADR-009

H. NASH Conversation Intelligence
OWNER OF: Conversation guidance, message framing, tone options, language suggestions.
NOT OWNER OF: Client intent truth, client decision, relationship permission, product truth, policy truth, recommendation authority.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Advisor Experience, NBA.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot invent client intent or product benefits.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-010

I. Relationship Intelligence
OWNER OF: Relationship context, timing signals, opportunity signals.
NOT OWNER OF: Permission to contact, permission to manipulate, client intent, client decision, commercial obligation, personal network inventory.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: NASH, NBA, Business Planning.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot treat relationship trust as permission for manipulation.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-011

J. Business Planning
OWNER OF: Action paths, goal progression.
NOT OWNER OF: Source truth, historical metric truth.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Manager Intelligence, Advisor Experience.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot treat action plans as confirmed results.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-012

K. Mick Behavior Intelligence
OWNER OF: Habit tracking, behavior patterns derived from repeated observable conduct.
NOT OWNER OF: Advisor human worth, internal motivations, raw activity logs, productivity truth.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Productivity, Manager Intelligence.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot diagnose advisor psychology based on latency.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-013

L. Productivity
OWNER OF: Operational KPIs, completion rates, productivity metrics derived from activity.
NOT OWNER OF: Human ranking, human worth, behavior intelligence, raw activity logs.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Manager Intelligence, Business Planning.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot be redefined by UI layers or be used as moral judgment.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-014

M. Manager Intelligence
OWNER OF: Coaching context, alert generation, team aggregation, manager-facing coaching interpretation.
NOT OWNER OF: Enforcement, direct discipline, metric definition.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Team/Manager UI, Business Planning.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot use Mick signals as punitive enforcement.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-015

N. Advisor Experience
OWNER OF: Explanation, contextual help, progressive discovery.
NOT OWNER OF: Business truth, metrics, policy states.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: End user presentation layer.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot simplify truth if doing so changes its meaning or hides uncertainty.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-016

O. Benvenù
OWNER OF: First-value experience, initial clarity, minimal validation request, capability-growth framing.
NOT OWNER OF: Core intelligence rules, CRM intake, corporate onboarding, forced setup.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Advisor Experience.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot create dependency or redefine system truth.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-016

P. Compensation Intelligence
OWNER OF: Rules of payment, commission structures, paid income (with payment evidence), earned but unpaid income.
NOT OWNER OF: Forecasts, premium issuance.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: Economic Motivation, Manager Intelligence.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot treat compensation scenarios as actual payments without evidence.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-017

Q. Economic Motivation
OWNER OF: Economic presentation posture, impact framing.
NOT OWNER OF: Economic truth, compensation facts.
ALLOWED CONSUMERS: NBA, Advisor Experience.
PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATIONS: Cannot redefine compensation reality or sacrifice Client First principles for advisor payout.
GOVERNING ADR: ADR-018

13. ALLOWED CONSUMPTION RULES

- Business Planning may consume Forecast scenarios but cannot convert them into facts.
- Manager Intelligence may consume Productivity metrics but cannot convert them into ranking or punishment.
- Advisor Experience may explain Product Truth but cannot simplify until truth changes.
- NBA may consume economic context but cannot prioritize payout over Client First.
- NASH may consume Relationship context but cannot manipulate or own intent.
- Economic Motivation may consume Compensation scenarios but cannot present them as real money.

14. PROHIBITED REINTERPRETATION RULES

Explicit prohibitions:
- Forecast cannot redefine Product Truth.
- Business Planning cannot redefine Metric Truth.
- Manager Intelligence cannot redefine Mick signals as diagnosis.
- Advisor Experience cannot redefine unknown as clarity.
- Economic Motivation cannot redefine Compensation Intelligence.
- NASH cannot redefine client intent.
- NBA cannot redefine recommendation as decision.
- Productivity cannot redefine human worth.
- Mick cannot redefine productivity truth.
- Compensation cannot redefine paid premium without payment evidence.
- Relationship Intelligence cannot redefine trust as permission.

15. OWNER UNKNOWN HANDLING

When owner is unknown:
- strong output blocked
- validation required
- human review required when sensitive
- unknown preserved
- no invented owner
- no local fallback owner unless explicitly allowed
- no downstream reinterpretation

16. OWNER CONFLICT HANDLING

When ownership conflicts:
- mark owner_conflicting
- list conflicting candidate owners
- block strong claim
- preserve uncertainty
- request ADR review if repeated
- require human review for sensitive domains
- do not silently pick owner

23. FINAL VERDICT

Source Ownership Registry is required before evidence validation because Forge cannot validate a claim if it does not know who owns the truth.

Ownership is not validity.
Consumption is not reinterpretation.
Presentation is not truth.

Unknown owner blocks strong output.
Conflicting owner blocks strong output.

Forge cannot safely build intelligent engines until source ownership is explicit, preserved and enforced.
