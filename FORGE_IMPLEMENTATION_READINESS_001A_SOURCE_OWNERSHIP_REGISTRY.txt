TITLE
FORGE IMPLEMENTATION READINESS 001A — SOURCE OWNERSHIP REGISTRY

STATUS
Implementation Readiness Blueprint

CONTEXT
The Forge OS Constitutional Phase is locked. Canonical ADRs (001-018) establish strict domain boundaries, metric ownership, and prohibition of invented data. The Source Ownership Registry translates these principles into conceptual rules. This prevents truth duplication, reinterpretation by presentation layers, and the misuse of forecasts, productivity metrics, and relationship signals. This document is self-contained and defines ownership rules without placeholders or drafts.

DECISION
Forge will enforce conceptual source ownership before any engine consumes or interprets data. Every source, evidence, metric, and truth claim must have an explicitly defined owner. Consumer domains may read but not redefine truth. Unknown or conflicting owners will block strong output claims.

DEFINITIONS
- Evidence Ownership: Validity of documents and raw logs.
- Product Truth: Official rules and limits from carrier docs.
- Policy Truth: Confirmed events, issued coverages, paid premiums.
- Economic Evidence: Validity of values, sums, quotes, and rates (UDI/FX).
- Forecast Truth: Scenarios and risk probabilities.
- Advisor Experience: First-value capability and explanation layer.
- Manager Intelligence: Coaching context and team alerts.
- Mick Behavior Signals: Observable habit patterns.
- Productivity Metrics: Quantitative KPIs, completion rates.
- NASH Conversation Intelligence: Messaging guidance and framing.
- NBA (Next Best Action): Prioritized action recommendations.
- Benvenù: First-value experience and capability growth.
- Relationship Intelligence: Relationship context and timing signals.
- Confidence Labels: Must be qualitative (e.g., alta, media, baja, provisional, validation needed, stale, conflicting, unknown). No numeric scoring.

OWNERSHIP RULES
- Evidence Ownership owns raw documents, OCR, raw activity logs, and unverified inputs.
- Product Truth owns carrier product rules.
- Policy Truth owns issued states and paid premiums (requiring payment evidence).
- Economic Evidence owns monetary values, UDI/FX sources, and raw quotes.
- Forecast Truth owns projected scenarios.
- Productivity owns metrics derived from activity.
- Mick owns behavior patterns derived from activity.
- Manager Intelligence owns coaching interpretations.
- NASH owns conversation guidance.
- NBA owns recommendation prioritization.
- Relationship Intelligence owns relationship opportunity signals.
- Benvenù owns first-value experience.
- Advisor Experience owns UI presentation.

VALID INPUTS
- Raw system-observed activity logs.
- Verified carrier documentation.
- Economic sources (UDI/FX official records).
- Payment evidence.
- Raw quote documents.
- Client-confirmed context.

INPUT BOUNDARIES
- Partial Evidence Handling: Unverified documents or missing context must result in provisional or unknown states.
- Quotes cannot be input as Policy Truth.
- Productivity inputs cannot include subjective human worth.
- UDI/FX inputs must come from official economic evidence, not product rules.
- Relationship signals cannot assume manipulation permission.

OUTPUTS PERMITIDOS
- "This productivity metric is below target for period X."
- "This risk scenario indicates potential cancellation."
- "This message is framed for relationship building."
- "This action is prioritized."
- "This economic value is valid."

OUTPUTS PROHIBIDOS
- "This advisor is low productivity." (No ranking, scoring, or evaluation of humans)
- "This policy will cancel." (Without policy evidence)
- "Send this message to close the sale." (No manipulation or intent assumption)
- "This manager must enforce this penalty." (No punitive enforcement)
- Any numeric confidence score representing human judgment.

DRIFT HANDLING
When observed data drifts from source truth:
- Strong claims are blocked.
- State reverts to 'conflicting' or 'validation needed'.
- Presentation layers must explain the uncertainty.
- No local fallback owner is allowed without explicit permission.

STALENESS HANDLING
When evidence or claims age beyond their validity period:
- State becomes 'stale'.
- Claims depending on stale evidence drop to 'validation needed'.
- Economic Evidence (UDI/FX) requires freshness checks before Forecast consumption.

AUTHORITY BOUNDARY
- Manager Intelligence advises, it does not enforce.
- NBA recommends, it does not decide.
- Productivity measures outputs, it does not judge human worth.
- NASH guides, it does not manipulate or own client intent.

CLIENT FIRST
All recommendations and intelligence outputs must prioritize client welfare over advisor compensation (ADR-018). Economic Motivation cannot pressure advisors into actions that violate client trust. Relationship Intelligence context cannot be used for spam or manipulation.

SURVIVAL TEST
If all UI layers fail, the Source Ownership Registry and evidence validation rules must independently prevent false claims, invented data, or incorrect ownership consumption by background intelligence engines.

ABSORPTION TEST
If a new source or metric is introduced, it must be absorbed strictly under one owner domain using the defined definitions and rules. Ambiguous metrics must trigger an ownership conflict state.

NON-GOALS
- Implementing code, schemas, databases, or workflows.
- Creating automation logic or engines.
- Designing UI or dashboards.
- Generating formulas for compensation or forecasting.
- Inventing commercial rules or human ranking systems.

FINAL VERDICT
This Source Ownership Registry is fully aligned with Forge OS Constitutional ADRs (001-018). It enforces strict domain separation, prevents truth duplication, treats productivity as metrics rather than human worth, and ensures all outputs adhere to Client First and Authority Boundary principles. Implementation readiness is confirmed.
