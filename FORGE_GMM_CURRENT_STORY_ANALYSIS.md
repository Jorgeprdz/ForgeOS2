## 2. Executive Summary

The current story is:

"Gastos Medicos Mayores is a product that gives protection, and here are the
main technical concepts you need to understand before seeing a quote."

The presentation is currently product-first and concept-first.

It does not begin with a human problem, financial consequence, fear, desire or
client story. It begins with product category, then moves into definitions:
suma asegurada, niveles hospitalarios, deducible, coaseguro, periodos de espera
and benefits.

The strongest narrative material appears late:

- slide 12: participation example in illness
- slide 13: participation example in accident
- slide 14: quote

These slides finally convert GMM from an abstract product into a financial
consequence. That is where the prospect can most clearly understand what
changes with or without GMM.

## 4. What Story Is Currently Being Told?

Current story:

"This is GMM. It protects you. To understand it, first learn the technical
components, then review examples, then review the quote."

This is structurally rational. It is not yet fully emotional.

The advisor is building understanding through definitions before urgency.

## 5. What Type of Opening Is Used?

The presentation begins with a product definition / product category.

It does not begin with:

- a problem
- a consequence
- a fear
- a desire
- a client scenario
- a financial shock

Slide 2 introduces tranquility, but only after the product has already been
named.

## 6. When Does the Prospect Understand "Why Should I Care?"

The prospect may intellectually understand the product by slide 2.

The prospect likely understands why they should care at slide 12 or slide 13.

Reason:

Slides 12 and 13 show the actual transformation:

- total medical expense
- client participation
- insurer participation
- difference between illness and accident

That is the first time the presentation makes the risk financially concrete.

## 7. Narrative Classification

Current classification:

- Product-first: high
- Concept-first: high
- Story-first: low
- Problem-first: low

The presentation is not a pure product brochure because it includes examples
and a quote, but its sequence asks the prospect to learn the product before
feeling the problem.

## 8. Current Narrative Strength

Strongest current narrative elements:

- protection / tranquility promise
- deductible and coinsurance concepts
- illness participation example
- accident participation example
- quote comparison

Weakest current narrative elements:

- lack of opening problem
- late arrival of financial consequence
- dense waiting-period section before emotional payoff
- limited contrast between life with GMM and life without GMM
- limited human scenario around accident or illness

## 9. Main Discovery

The presentation already contains the core conversion material.

The issue is not absence of value.

The issue is narrative order.

The most memorable and persuasive elements appear after the prospect has already
passed through several technical slides.
