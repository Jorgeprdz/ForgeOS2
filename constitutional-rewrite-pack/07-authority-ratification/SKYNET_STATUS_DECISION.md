# SKYNET Constitutional Status Decision

Status: DISCOVERY / NOT RATIFIED / NO AUTHORITY

Definitive discovery recommendation: `RATIFY WITH CONDITIONS`

The recommendation is documented in `SKYNET_CONSTITUTIONAL_DISCOVERY.md`. It has no ratifying effect: the status on this page remains unchanged until explicit human constitutional ratification and all recorded conditions are closed.

## Decision

SKYNET is not ratified by the available evidence.

All four SKYNET source documents explicitly state `ARCHITECTURE DISCOVERY`, `NOT RATIFIED`, `NO IMPLEMENTATION` or equivalent language. The original repository contains no ratified SKYNET law artifact. References saying “Skynet remains unchanged” preserve non-modification; they do not perform ratification.

## Permitted meaning

SKYNET may remain a discovery codename for a proposed umbrella of cross-domain constitutional constraints.

It is not:

- an AI;
- an engine;
- an intelligence domain;
- a runtime validator;
- a source of truth;
- an approval authority;
- a currently enforceable independent law pack.

## Relationship to existing authority

Its strongest law candidates are already substantially governed by ratified authority:

- Evidence Before Judgment: Article 0, ADR-001 and ADR-004.
- Human Dignity: Article 0, ADR-003, ADR-011, ADR-015 and ADR-016.
- Purpose Is Context, Not Pressure: ADR-016A candidate and ADR-018, but the dedicated purpose addendum remains unclosed.
- Production Is Evidence, Not Proof: ADR-001, ADR-002, ADR-007, ADR-008 and ADR-017.

SKYNET may not be used to create a second or higher copy of those rules without explicit constitutional ratification.

## What is missing for ratification

1. Explicit human constitutional ratification.
2. A versioned, finite list of exact laws.
3. Conflict and amendment rules reconciled with Article 0, AX-001, the Constitution and ADRs.
4. A declaration of whether SKYNET adds new authority or is only a navigation label.
5. A non-duplication analysis against locked ADRs.
6. Testable consequences for violations without creating autonomous execution authority.
7. Governance registry and Constitution Map incorporation.

## Required document type

If retained, SKYNET requires a constitutional amendment or explicit law-ratification record. It does not require an implementation ADR merely to become governance. Any later enforcement mechanism would require its own ADR and specifications.
