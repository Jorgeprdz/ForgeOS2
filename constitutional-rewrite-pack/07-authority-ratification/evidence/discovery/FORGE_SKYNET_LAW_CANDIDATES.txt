FORGE SKYNET LAW CANDIDATES

Status:
ARCHITECTURE DISCOVERY / NOT RATIFIED

Scope:
No implementation. No code. No engines. No schemas. No Build Tree changes.

IMPORTANT:
These are law candidates only. None are ratified by this document.

QUALIFICATION TEST

A Skynet Law candidate should:
- apply across multiple intelligence systems
- prevent serious harm
- constrain interpretation and recommendation
- remain valid as domains evolve
- be universal or nearly universal
- be testable as a boundary
- block unsafe outputs when violated

CANDIDATE LAW 1

Evidence Before Judgment.

Meaning:
No intelligence may produce a judgment, classification, recommendation or
development interpretation without evidence, confidence and source context.

Supported by:
- Career Stage discovery
- Professional Excellence discovery
- Professional Development discovery
- Readiness discovery
- Constitutional consolidation

Why it may qualify:
It is universal and protects against invented career, behavior, financial and
human conclusions.

Risks if absent:
- invented readiness
- invented excellence
- manager bias automated as truth
- confidence without source
- AI explanation pretending uncertainty is fact

Lock readiness:
STRONG CANDIDATE

CANDIDATE LAW 2

Human Dignity Must Be Preserved.

Meaning:
No intelligence may reduce a person to a score, label, failure state or moral
judgment.

Supported by:
- Professional Excellence boundaries
- Professional Development boundaries
- Readiness boundaries
- Manager Intelligence boundaries

Why it may qualify:
Every Forge intelligence that interprets people must preserve dignity.

Risks if absent:
- surveillance
- shame
- simplistic ranking
- "not ready" treated as failure
- excellence treated as human worth

Lock readiness:
STRONG CANDIDATE

CANDIDATE LAW 3

Purpose Is Context, Not Pressure.

Meaning:
No intelligence may use purpose, family drivers, ambition or personal context to
pressure, shame, coerce or manipulate a person.

Supported by:
- Benvenù
- Future You Conversation
- Purpose Snapshot
- Professional Development
- Professional Excellence
- Readiness
- SMNYL contextual truth

Why it may qualify:
Purpose data is sensitive and will influence multiple domains.

Risks if absent:
- motivation scoring
- sales pressure
- manager coercion
- emotional manipulation

Lock readiness:
STRONG CANDIDATE

CANDIDATE LAW 4

Production Is Evidence, Not Proof.

Meaning:
No intelligence may treat production, revenue or output as complete proof of
stage, excellence, development, readiness, maturity or leadership quality.

Supported by:
- Career Stage
- Professional Excellence
- Professional Development
- Readiness
- Compensation/Forecast boundaries

Why it may qualify:
Production-only interpretation is one of the most likely and dangerous
shortcuts in a commercial system.

Risks if absent:
- premature promotion
- false excellence
- ignored trust risk
- compensation mistaken for maturity

Lock readiness:
STRONG CANDIDATE

CANDIDATE LAW 5

No Single Score Represents Human Growth.

Meaning:
No intelligence may claim that one score fully represents professional growth,
excellence, development, readiness, potential or human value.

Supported by:
- Excellence discovery
- Development discovery
- Readiness discovery
- Constitutional lock preparation

Why it may qualify:
Forge will likely need summaries, but must avoid false precision and ranking
theater.

Risks if absent:
- leaderboard culture
- single-number manager pressure
- hidden weighting
- reduction of complex development into a simplistic metric

Lock readiness:
STRONG CANDIDATE
