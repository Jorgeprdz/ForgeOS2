### Origin and problem

Andrey appeared as Seat 13 to address allocation of finite organizational development resources: manager time, training capacity and tool access. FCA-1.3 framed this as Talent Intelligence/Future Capacity and attempted to protect recruitment and development investment. The current Constitution preserves the narrower Human Capital Allocation/Resource Scarcity purpose.

### Ratification finding

FCA-1.3 is an explicit historical ratification, and the current Constitution incorporates its seat under revised terminology. The evidence is stronger than a proposal. However, FCA-1.3's operational flows were not wholly preserved: later ratified Article 0 and locked ADRs invalidate automatic veto, fixed-potential judgment, worthiness, opaque scoring and final human consequences.

## 2. Constitutional Status

Andrey is ratified as a restricted Council seat and constitutional Human Capital Allocation assessment domain. The ratification is not partial as to existence; it is restricted as to authority.

- Not merely discovered: FCA-1.3 explicitly transitioned the seat out of discovery.
- Not abandoned: the current Constitution and Build Tree retain it.
- Not replaced: no later authority replaces Human Capital Allocation.
- Terminology and authority were degraded/narrowed: “Talent Authority,” prediction, worthiness and automatic veto are superseded by evidence-bound allocation assessment and human decision authority.
- Runtime remains unratified and blocked.

## 3. Constitutional Purpose

Andrey's constitutional purpose is to preserve accountable human allocation of finite development resources by organizing valid, contextual evidence and uncertainty for authorized manager review.

This purpose protects resource stewardship. It does not define human value, predict destiny or decide who deserves development.

## 4. Constitutional Problem and Non-Duplication

The gap is ownership of the manager-facing question: “What evidence should inform allocation of finite development resources, and what remains unknown?”

## 5. Domain

### Governs

- the conceptual composition of an evidence-bound development-resource allocation assessment;
- disclosure of missing, stale, conflicting and unknown allocation evidence;
- separation of current observations from scenarios and assumptions;
- the threshold at which Forge may present an allocation assessment as reviewable, not the threshold at which a person deserves investment.

### Never governs

- human worth, character, identity, innate greatness, destiny or fixed potential;
- hiring, contracting, firing, promotion, demotion, punishment or labor evaluation;
- candidate, recruitment, Mick, productivity, compensation, relationship or policy truth;
- Advisor OS communication, Nash strategy or Russell identity;
- private purpose, protected attributes or psychological diagnosis;
- final allocation decisions or execution.

Authority begins when an authorized manager requests manager-facing allocation context supported by owned evidence. It ends at an explainable assessment/review signal. The accountable manager makes the decision within applicable law and organizational policy.

## 6. Responsibilities

## 7. Authority

Andrey may emit evidence summaries, evidence-gap warnings, allocation assessment candidates, alternatives and `HUMAN_REVIEW_REQUIRED` or `NO_CONCLUSION_POSSIBLE` states.

Andrey cannot approve, veto or block a legitimate human decision; cannot alter source results; cannot execute resource allocation; and cannot impose consequences. It may block only its own invalid system output when evidence or constitutional boundaries fail.

The manager can reject or correct an assessment. Source owners can invalidate its inputs. Miranda/ROBOCOP can hold a nonconforming system output/change. Board or explicit constitutional authority can amend or revoke the seat through recorded governance; Andrey cannot defend or expand itself.

## 8. Relationship with Nash

Nash produces conversation interpretation, objection handling and next-best-action guidance from authorized conversation/domain context. Andrey produces manager-facing allocation assessment context.

Neither is a required input to the other. Andrey must not decide whether a person is “worth the message,” change Nash strategy, gate communication or consume private conversation content merely to judge potential. Nash must not translate an Andrey assessment into pressure, discriminatory treatment or advisor-facing judgment.

Conflicts resolve in favor of source ownership, ADR-003 human authority, ADR-010/011 conversation and non-manipulation limits, privacy and `NO_CONCLUSION_POSSIBLE`.

## 9. Relationship with Mick

Mick owns observable behavior signals and their limitations. Andrey may consume an official Mick signal only as one bounded input when purpose, consent, period and context authorize it.

Andrey does not influence behavior, generate behavior plans, score discipline/coachability or reinterpret activity as talent. It organizes evidence for manager review; Manager Intelligence and the human manager own coaching context and decisions.

## 10. Relationship with Miranda

Miranda validates scope discipline, evidence visibility, uncertainty, language, non-manipulation, Article 0 compatibility, Advisor/Manager separation and absence of hidden authority expansion.

Miranda Approval is required before any Andrey implementation, interface, metric, weighting, scoring, privacy-sensitive processing, manager-facing presentation or authority change. The historical terminology review approves “assessment” as safer wording; it is not blanket approval of runtime, metrics or decisions.

Miranda cannot authorize automatic veto, waive locked ADRs or ratify Andrey's own expansion.

## 11. Relationship with SKYNET

No SKYNET Law currently applies because SKYNET and all candidates remain unratified. Current constraints come directly from Article 0, Constitution and locked ADRs.

If SKYNET becomes active, Evidence Before Judgment and Human Dignity would apply to Andrey outputs. A conflict would invalidate/degrade the system output, request evidence or require human review; Andrey could not invoke confidence to bypass the law. SKYNET would restrict Andrey, never supply allocation truth or decide for the manager.

## 12. Human Threshold

“Human Threshold” is rejected as a threshold of human quality, worth, potential or eligibility.

The only constitutionally valid meaning is **Allocation Assessment Evidence Threshold**: the minimum sufficiency of authorized, relevant, current and contextual evidence required before Forge may present a manager-facing allocation assessment for human review.

It is not a universal number or score. Until a ratified authority-boundary ADR defines inputs and PAQ, it is measured qualitatively:

- source and conceptual owner identified;
- period/context and Rule Pack, if applicable, identified;
- evidence state known;
- conflicts, staleness and missing evidence exposed;
- protected/private data excluded or authorized;
- no prohibited human inference;
- accountable human review present.

It must never infer character, moral discipline, identity, intrinsic talent, fixed potential, employability, worthiness or future success.

## 13. Evidence

### Permitted sources

- official source-owned lifecycle and recruitment facts;
- current Productivity outputs with ownership/period/context;
- bounded Mick behavior signals;
- manager- or advisor-confirmed facts labeled as such;
- official goals, assignments, training capacity and resource constraints;
- applicable Organization Profile and Rule Pack facts;
- historical outcomes used as context, never destiny.

### Forbidden sources

- protected traits or proxies used for discrimination;
- private purpose, hidden communications or unauthorized Advisor OS data;
- inferred personality, character, mental state or moral worth;
- rumors, popularity, social surveillance or unowned scores;
- AI-generated facts, stale data presented as current, or forecasts presented as truth;
- Russell identity/drift proposals or private relationship content.

Minimum evidence requires source, owner, purpose, period, freshness, consent/access basis, evidence state, uncertainty and relevance. Bias is limited through source diversity, prohibited-proxy review, cohort/context limits, missing-data disclosure, no single-score decisions, correction rights and periodic human audit. These controls are conceptual; implementation remains blocked pending canonical policy and ADR.

## 14. Privacy

Andrey may conceptually retain only necessary source references, evidence states, period/context, assessment rationale, human review outcome and audit/version lineage under an approved retention policy.

It may not retain private purpose, psychological profiles, hidden communications, protected traits, inferred character, fixed-potential labels, human-worth scores or indefinite raw behavioral histories.

Explicit authorization is required for personal profiling, cross-domain use, manager access and reuse beyond the original operational purpose. Manager-facing outputs must minimize identity and expose only necessary evidence. Team analysis must be aggregated/anonymized when individual identity is unnecessary. The data-governance, consent, retention and deletion contracts are absent; therefore no persistence is authorized.

## 15. Conceptual Interfaces

### Inputs

An `AllocationReviewContext`: accountable manager, subject/case reference, requested resource, decision horizon, authorized source references, evidence states, period, applicable rules, consent/access state and known constraints.

### Outputs

An `AllocationAssessment`: supported observations, unsupported claims removed, alternatives, evidence gaps, uncertainty, applicable limits, `HUMAN_REVIEW_REQUIRED`/`NO_CONCLUSION_POSSIBLE`, and immutable source/version references.

### Events

Consumes conceptual evidence-confirmed/corrected/staled/conflicted and resource-context-updated events. Emits assessment-available, evidence-required, assessment-invalidated and human-review-required events. Event names are conceptual and do not authorize schemas.

### Consumers/dependencies

Only authorized Manager Intelligence/review surfaces and governance audit may consume the assessment. Dependencies are evidence owners, privacy/consent policy, Organization Profile/Rule Pack where relevant, Article 0, ADRs, Miranda and human authority. Nash, Russell and Advisor OS are not consumers.

## 19. Constitutional Lock Readiness

**Sí.**

Evidence is sufficient to lock the restricted constitutional existence and boundary because:

1. FCA-1.3 explicitly performs operational ratification.
2. The current Constitution independently incorporates Andrey and HCA after the rewrite.
3. The current Build Tree corroborates Ratified Seat 13.
4. Article 0 and locked ADRs provide objective, later constraints that reconcile the unsafe historical authority.
5. The lock can preserve history without authorizing runtime, metrics, veto or human consequences.

The evidence is not sufficient for implementation readiness. That is a separate gate and remains blocked.

## 20. Final Decision

**`RATIFY AND LOCK`**

Meaning: recognize the already-ratified Seat 13 and lock its only constitutionally surviving scope as an evidence-bound, manager-facing Human Capital Allocation assessment authority. This does not re-ratify historical overreach.

The lock removes ambiguity by making explicit that:

- existence/domain are `LOCKED`;
- implementation/runtime are unauthorized;
- automatic veto/approval and human-potential judgment are prohibited;
- “Talent Authority” and “Human Threshold” are superseded terminology;
- source owners, Manager Intelligence and humans retain their authority;
- any implementation requires a new HCA Authority Boundary ADR, privacy contracts, PAQ, Miranda Approval and applicable Board Approval.

Constitutional impact: preserves the current Constitution while bringing FCA-1.3 under Article 0 and ADR-003/013/014/015.

Architectural impact: establishes a narrow future contract seam inside Manager Intelligence and forbids dependencies on Nash, Russell or Advisor OS.

Rewrite Pack impact: add this review and the lock; supersede the earlier derived closure as navigation, update inventory, authority matrix and validation report. No Constitution, ADR or Build Tree modification.
