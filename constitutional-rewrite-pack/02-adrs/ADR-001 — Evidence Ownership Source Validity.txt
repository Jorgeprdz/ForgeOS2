ADR-001 — EVIDENCE OWNERSHIP / SOURCE VALIDITY

1. TITLE

ADR-001 — Evidence Ownership / Source Validity

2. STATUS

Proposed / Draft

3. CONTEXT

Forge OS conectará múltiples dominios, fuentes, documentos, productos, pólizas, métricas, conversaciones, forecasts, managers, asesores y acciones.

Para que Forge funcione como Decision Intelligence confiable, cada interpretación, recomendación, priorización, forecast, explicación o salida importante debe estar sustentada por evidencia reconocible y limitada.

Sin reglas de ownership y validez de evidencia, Forge puede:

- inventar hechos
- mezclar fuentes
- presentar inferencias como verdad
- usar evidencia débil como certeza
- duplicar owners
- crear recomendaciones sin base
- contaminar Forecast, NBA, NASH, Product Intelligence, Policy Intelligence, Compensation Intelligence y Manager Intelligence
- convertir lenguaje generado por IA en aparente evidencia
- ocultar incertidumbre
- presentar unknown como dato

Este ADR deriva directamente de Forge Constitution v1.0 y Forge Constitutional Commentary v1.0.

Principios aplicables:

- Evidence First
- Decision Clarity First
- Client First
- Authority Boundary
- Advisor Capability Growth
- Causal Clarity

4. DECISION

Forge debe conectar toda interpretación, recomendación, priorización, forecast, explicación o salida importante a:

- evidencia identificable
- fuente válida
- owner conceptual
- contexto
- periodo
- incertidumbre
- límites de inferencia

Unknown remains unknown.

Cuando Forge no tenga evidencia suficiente, debe conservar lo desconocido como desconocido.

Forge puede operar con evidencia parcial, pero debe declarar su límite y no presentar conclusiones fuertes como si fueran hechos confirmados.

5. DEFINITIONS

Evidence

Información identificable que puede sustentar una afirmación, interpretación, recomendación, forecast, explicación o decisión dentro de Forge.

Source

Origen de la evidencia.

Puede ser documento, registro, regla, evento observado, confirmación humana, fuente oficial externa validada o historial conversacional con contexto.

Source Validity

Grado en que una fuente es apropiada, vigente, contextual y confiable para sostener una afirmación específica.

Evidence Owner

Dominio, regla o contexto conceptual autorizado para interpretar una evidencia como verdad dentro de su boundary.

El owner no es necesariamente quien subió el archivo ni quien muestra la evidencia.

Consumer Domain

Dominio que usa evidencia o interpretaciones oficiales de otro owner para producir su propia salida sin redefinir la verdad original.

Derived Interpretation

Conclusión, lectura o significado producido a partir de evidencia existente.

Debe conservar trazabilidad a la evidencia base.

Fact

Afirmación confirmada por evidencia suficiente y fuente válida dentro de un contexto definido.

Assumption

Supuesto usado para razonar cuando falta confirmación completa.

Debe etiquetarse como supuesto y no como hecho.

Inference

Conclusión razonable derivada de evidencia, pero no confirmada directamente.

Debe expresar incertidumbre.

Forecast

Escenario, proyección, estimación o expectativa futura basada en evidencia y supuestos.

Forecast no es hecho.

Recommendation

Acción sugerida por Forge con base en evidencia, contexto, prioridad y límites.

Una recomendación no es decisión final.

Unknown

Dato, estado, valor, intención, regla o contexto que Forge no puede confirmar con evidencia suficiente.

Confidence

Nivel cualitativo de confianza asociado a una interpretación, inferencia, recomendación o forecast.

Este ADR no crea scores de confidence.

Evidence Boundary

Límite que define qué puede y qué no puede afirmarse con la evidencia disponible.

6. VALID EVIDENCE TYPES

Uploaded Official Documents

Documentos oficiales cargados por usuario o fuente autorizada.

Limitación:
Deben considerarse dentro de versión, fecha, contexto, carrier, producto y vigencia.

Policy Documents

Documentos de póliza, estados, renovaciones, endosos o eventos operativos.

Limitación:
Pueden sostener Policy Truth, pero deben verificarse contra contexto y vigencia.

Product Manuals

Manuales, condiciones, fichas, guías o documentación oficial de producto.

Limitación:
No deben mezclarse productos, versiones, carriers o periodos.

Quotes / Illustrations

Cotizaciones, ilustraciones, propuestas o simulaciones.

Limitación:
No son póliza emitida ni ingreso confirmado. Pueden caducar o depender de supuestos.

Rule Packs

Reglas configuradas para carrier, canal, compensación, concurso, carrera o contexto operativo.

Limitación:
Deben aplicarse solo dentro de su periodo, snapshot y contexto.

CRM Records

Registros de clientes, prospectos, tareas, actividades, oportunidades o eventos.

Limitación:
Pueden estar incompletos, desactualizados o depender de captura manual.

User-Entered Data

Datos capturados por asesor, manager u otro usuario.

Limitación:
No siempre son confirmados. Deben distinguirse de datos document-backed o system-observed.

Advisor-Confirmed Facts

Hechos confirmados explícitamente por el asesor.

Limitación:
Son confirmaciones humanas, no necesariamente evidencia documental.

Manager-Confirmed Facts

Hechos confirmados explícitamente por manager.

Limitación:
No deben confundirse con evaluación final, consecuencia laboral o verdad documental.

System Logs / Observed Behavior

Eventos observados por Forge: acciones, tiempos, completions, cambios, cadencias o uso.

Limitación:
Observan comportamiento externo, no motivación interna.

Conversation History

Historial conversacional registrado con contexto.

Limitación:
No debe inferir intención interna, emoción o consentimiento sin evidencia explícita.

External Official Sources When Explicitly Validated

Fuentes oficiales externas validadas para el contexto.

Limitación:
Deben distinguirse de fuentes web genéricas, contenido no oficial o información no versionada.

7. INVALID OR WEAK EVIDENCE

No debe tratarse como evidencia fuerte:

AI-Generated Text Without Source

Texto generado por IA sin fuente verificable no es evidencia.

Inferred Client Intent Without Confirmation

La intención del cliente no debe asumirse sin confirmación explícita o evidencia suficiente.

Outdated Documents Without Version Awareness

Documentos desactualizados no deben usarse como verdad vigente sin revisión de versión.

Screenshots Without Context

Screenshots pueden servir como indicio, pero no como verdad fuerte si carecen de fecha, fuente, owner o contexto.

Memory Without Source

Memoria no trazable no debe funcionar como evidencia fuerte.

Forecast Treated As Fact

Forecast es escenario, no hecho.

Manager Opinion Treated As Fact

Opinión de manager puede ser señal, no verdad final.

Advisor Assumption Treated As Fact

Supuesto del asesor puede orientar validación, no reemplazar evidencia.

Client Silence Treated As Intent

Silencio del cliente no debe interpretarse automáticamente como rechazo, interés o consentimiento.

Correlation Treated As Causation

Correlación no debe presentarse como causalidad.

8. OWNERSHIP RULES

Toda evidencia debe tener owner conceptual cuando aplique.

Un dominio puede consumir evidencia, pero no redefinir la verdad de otro owner.

Owner no significa:

- usuario que subió el archivo
- dominio que muestra la evidencia
- pantalla donde aparece
- engine que la consume

Owner significa:

dominio, regla o contexto autorizado para interpretar esa evidencia como verdad dentro de un boundary.

Ejemplos:

Product Intelligence owns Product Truth.

Policy Intelligence owns Policy Truth.

Compensation Intelligence owns compensation rule interpretation.

Mick owns observable behavior patterns.

Productivity owns activity-to-output interpretation.

Forecast consumes facts but owns scenarios, not facts.

Business Planning consumes official facts, metrics and forecasts to create action paths, but does not redefine source truth.

NASH consumes relationship, product, policy and context evidence to produce conversation guidance, but does not invent product truth or policy truth.

Relationship Intelligence owns relationship opportunities and relationship context, but does not own product, policy or compensation truth.

Manager Intelligence consumes evidence from other domains to support coaching and development, but does not redefine metric truth or automate consequences.

9. EVIDENCE STATES

Forge may classify evidence conceptually as:

Confirmed

Evidence sufficiently supported within its proper source and context.

Advisor-Confirmed

Confirmed by advisor statement or explicit advisor input.

Manager-Confirmed

Confirmed by manager statement or explicit manager input.

Client-Confirmed

Confirmed by client statement, consent, response or documented client input.

Document-Backed

Supported by document evidence.

System-Observed

Observed directly through Forge logs or system activity.

Inferred

Derived from evidence, but not directly confirmed.

Provisional

Usable with limits pending stronger confirmation.

Stale

Potentially outdated due to age, version, period or changed context.

Conflicting

Evidence conflicts with another source or owner.

Unknown

Not confirmed with sufficient evidence.

10. PARTIAL EVIDENCE HANDLING

Forge may operate with partial evidence.

With partial evidence, Forge may:

- suggest hypotheses
- ask for confirmation
- recommend a validation step
- produce a provisional recommendation
- explain uncertainty
- identify what evidence is missing

With partial evidence, Forge may not:

- present a strong conclusion
- hide uncertainty
- convert assumption into fact
- treat provisional interpretation as confirmed truth
- recommend high-impact action without limitation
- invent missing values

11. UNKNOWN HANDLING

Unknown remains unknown.

Unknown may generate:

- a question
- a validation task
- a lower-confidence recommendation
- a blocked strong recommendation
- a request for source
- a provisional interpretation
- a safe next step to gather evidence

Unknown may not become:

- invented fact
- invented value
- invented recommendation
- hidden assumption
- fake confidence
- implied consent
- implied intent

12. CROSS-DOMAIN CONSUMPTION

Product Intelligence

May consume:
official product documents, manuals, rule packs, validated product data.

May not redefine:
policy status, compensation outcomes, client intent or forecast.

Policy Intelligence

May consume:
policy documents, operational records, renewal data, event history.

May not redefine:
product truth, compensation rules or advisor behavior patterns.

Forecast

May consume:
official facts, production data, policy data, productivity metrics, business planning context.

May not redefine:
facts, paid income, policy status, compensation truth or production truth.

Forecast owns scenarios, not facts.

NBA

May consume:
evidence-backed recommendations, relationship context, business priorities, policy/product facts, forecast scenarios and action candidates.

May not redefine:
source truth, client intent, product truth, policy truth or authority boundary.

NASH

May consume:
conversation history, relationship context, product truth, policy truth, objections, intent signals and recommendation context.

May not invent:
products, benefits, premiums, client intent, coverage, policy facts or recommendations without source.

Relationship Intelligence

May consume:
CRM records, engagement history, life events, client interactions, referral context and relationship activity.

May not redefine:
product suitability, compensation, forecast truth or client consent.

Manager Intelligence

May consume:
behavior evidence, productivity metrics, development signals, business planning context and performance indicators.

May not redefine:
human worth, final employment conclusions, metric truth or advisor intent.

Business Planning

May consume:
goals, forecast scenarios, productivity metrics, compensation context, relationship opportunities and known constraints.

May not redefine:
official metrics, policy truth, product truth or forecast as fact.

Mick

May consume:
system-observed behavior, activity patterns, task completion, cadence and consistency signals.

May not infer:
personality, motivation, character, emotion or internal intent without evidence.

Productivity

May consume:
activity records, conversion data, throughput, outcomes and official metrics.

May not redefine:
behavioral intent, relationship quality, product truth or compensation truth.

Compensation Intelligence

May consume:
production events, rule packs, compensation schedules, snapshots, periods and official economic evidence.

May not invent:
income, commission, payout, qualification or economic outcomes.

Economic Motivation

May consume:
compensation context, business goals, income scenarios and evidence-backed economic signals.

May not override:
Client First, product suitability, advisor authority or unknown handling.

Advisor Experience

May consume:
learning state, feature usage, context, advisor progression, help signals and system behavior.

May not redefine:
business truth, product truth, policy truth, compensation truth, metric truth or recommendations as mandates.

13. PROHIBITIONS

Forge must not:

- invent evidence
- hide source
- mix evidence owners
- present inference as fact
- present forecast as fact
- convert unknown into data
- use weak evidence as certainty
- generate recommendation without sufficient evidence
- allow a consumer domain to redefine truth
- use AI language as substitute for evidence
- treat correlation as causation
- infer client intent from silence
- infer advisor motivation from behavior alone
- treat manager opinion as confirmed fact
- merge historical periods without rule context
- mix rule packs without explicit boundary
- present provisional interpretation as confirmed truth

14. CONSEQUENCES

Positive Consequences

This ADR creates:

- greater traceability
- lower hallucination risk
- stronger Evidence First enforcement
- foundation for One Metric One Owner
- foundation for Product Truth
- foundation for Policy Truth
- foundation for Forecast Truth Boundary
- foundation for Recommendation Validity
- foundation for reliable NBA
- foundation for responsible NASH
- foundation for fair Manager Intelligence
- stronger unknown handling
- clearer domain boundaries

Tradeoffs

This ADR may introduce:

- more initial friction
- more need for metadata
- more visible unknowns
- fewer “magical” answers
- more validation work
- slower recommendations in weak-evidence contexts
- stricter boundaries between domains
- less tolerance for persuasive but unsupported output

These tradeoffs are acceptable because Forge must remain a reliable Decision Intelligence System.

17. FINAL VERDICT

ADR-001 establishes that Forge cannot operate as reliable Decision Intelligence without evidence ownership and source validity.

Evidence First becomes operational through this ADR.

Forge may interpret, recommend, prioritize, forecast and explain only when it can identify the evidence, source, owner, context, period, uncertainty and limits that support the output.

Unknown remains unknown.
