ADR-018 — ECONOMIC MOTIVATION CLIENT FIRST BOUNDARY

1. TITLE

ADR-018 — Economic Motivation Client First Boundary

2. STATUS

Final

3. CONTEXT

Forge ya cuenta con ADR-001 a ADR-017.

ADR-018 define cómo Forge puede usar contexto económico, metas de ingreso, escenarios de compensación, motivación financiera, business planning, gaps económicos y objetivos comerciales para ayudar al asesor sin crear ansiedad, presión indebida, payout-first behavior, manipulación del cliente o violación de Client First.

Economic Motivation existe para responder:

¿Cómo puede Forge presentar implicaciones económicas, motivación financiera y escenarios de ingreso sin inducir presión indebida, ansiedad, manipulación o recomendaciones payout-first?

Economic Motivation no responde:

- qué valor económico existe sin evidencia
- qué compensación está confirmada
- qué producto debe recomendarse por comisión
- qué cliente debe ser presionado por ingreso del asesor
- qué NBA debe convertirse en orden
- qué manager debe presionar al asesor
- qué relación debe explotarse para cerrar un gap
- qué ingreso está garantizado
- qué asesor vale más por dinero

Sin Economic Motivation Client First Boundary, Forge puede:

- convertir income goal en presión al cliente
- convertir compensation scenario en promesa
- convertir economic gap en mandato comercial
- convertir productividad en vergüenza económica
- convertir manager context en presión jerárquica
- convertir relationship context en explotación
- convertir NBA en orden por dinero
- convertir NASH en script manipulativo
- convertir payout en razón de recomendación
- subordinar Client First al ingreso del asesor
- ocultar uncertainty para motivar
- presentar dinero proyectado como dinero real
- crear anxiety-driven sales
- degradar Advisor Experience con miedo económico

ADR-018 protege Client First cuando dinero, metas o compensación entran en la experiencia.

4. DECISION

Economic Motivation es el owner conceptual de la presentación responsable de contexto económico motivacional para el asesor.

Economic Motivation puede explicar escenarios económicos, gaps de ingreso, tradeoffs, metas económicas y contexto de compensación solo como información limitada, evidencia-aware y Client First.

Economic Motivation no puede crear evidencia económica, compensation truth, forecast truth, business planning truth, NBA priority, NASH language final, manager enforcement o relationship permission.

Economic Motivation no puede usar dinero como palanca de manipulación.

Money is context, not pressure.

Income goal is direction, not mandate.

Compensation scenario is not promise.

Economic gap is not client pressure.

Client First remains above economic motivation.

5. DEFINITIONS

Economic Motivation

Dominio responsable de presentar contexto económico al asesor de forma responsable, limitada, evidence-aware y Client First.

Economic Motivation Context

Explicación de cómo un valor económico, escenario, meta, gap o tradeoff puede orientar reflexión o planeación sin presionar.

Income Goal

Meta de ingreso declarada, oficial o personal. No equivale a ingreso real ni justifica presión comercial.

Economic Gap

Diferencia entre una meta económica y un estado actual o escenario. No es mandato.

Compensation Scenario Explanation

Explicación motivacional de un escenario de compensación provisto por Compensation Intelligence, preservando límites y uncertainty.

Payout-First Behavior

Conducta donde payout, comisión, bono o ingreso se prioriza sobre suitability, Client First, evidencia o autoridad humana.

Financial Anxiety

Estado de presión, miedo o angustia económica que puede inducir decisiones comerciales inseguras.

Fear-Based UX

Experiencia que usa miedo económico para empujar acción.

Shame Prompt

Mensaje que usa vergüenza económica, productividad o comparación para presionar al asesor.

Client First Reminder

Advertencia explícita de que el interés, autonomía, suitability y confianza del cliente permanecen por encima de ingreso, comisión o meta.

No Economic Motivation Possible

Estado donde no existe evidencia, contexto o límite suficiente para presentar motivación económica responsable.

Motivation Blocked by Missing Evidence

Estado donde Forge bloquea motivación económica fuerte porque falta evidencia económica, compensation context, forecast limit, plan context o Client First boundary.

It does not redefine the source domain.

8. INPUT BOUNDARIES

Economic Evidence

Can provide valid economic values, currency, period, conversion, state and limits.

Must not be reinterpreted as motivation without preserving uncertainty.

Compensation Intelligence outputs

Can provide paid, earned, estimated, projected, provisional or unknown compensation context.

Must not become promise, pressure or product recommendation.

Forecast scenarios

Can provide future context.

Must remain scenario and cannot become real income.

Business Planning outputs

Can provide routes, gaps, constraints and review loops.

Must not become mandate because of economic motivation.

Productivity Metrics

Can explain activity/income tradeoffs when evidence supports it.

Must not become shame or human worth.

Manager Intelligence context

Can provide coaching context.

Must not become manager pressure loop.

Advisor Experience context

Can help adjust explanation clarity.

Must not hide uncertainty to increase adoption.

Advisor-stated income goals

Can provide personal context.

Must not become official goal or client pressure.

Official goals and campaign goals

Can provide objective context.

Must preserve source, period and rules.

CRM records

Can provide candidate context.

Must not replace Economic Evidence or Compensation Intelligence.

Policy Truth and Product Truth

Can provide client/product/policy facts.

Must not be subordinated to payout.

Relationship Intelligence context

Can provide relational timing.

Must not convert relationships into inventory for economic gaps.

NASH outputs

Can provide conversational draft context.

Must not become economic pressure script.

NBA outputs

Can provide action priority context.

Must not become mandate because of money.

Advisor-confirmed and manager-confirmed context

Can clarify context.

Must not become economic truth, payment evidence or client suitability.

Constraints, evidence gaps and uncertainty states

Must be preserved and surfaced.

They must not be hidden for motivation.

- economic context explanation

- Revisa esta oportunidad solo si también cumple Client First.

- product recommendation

- manipulation sequence

- relationship exploitation

Prohibited examples:

- Este bono está garantizado.

11. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION STATES

Economic Motivation may produce these states:

- economic motivation available
- economic motivation limited
- validation needed

- no economic motivation possible
- motivation blocked by missing evidence
- scenario under assumptions
- uncertainty warning

- payout-first risk

These states describe motivation safety and evidence posture.

They do not create recommendation, mandate, payment or suitability.

12. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION CONFIDENCE

Economic Motivation may express qualitative confidence only:

- alta confianza contextual
- confianza moderada
- confianza baja
- provisional

- conflicting
- unknown

Forbidden:

- Motivation Score
- Payout Priority Score
- Client Pressure Score
- Advisor Shame Score
- Manager Pressure Score
- Urgency Manipulation Score
- Product by Commission Score

Confidence describes evidence and presentation safety.

It does not describe human value, product suitability or income guarantee.

13. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS ECONOMIC EVIDENCE

Economic Evidence answers:

What economic values have evidence, source, currency, period, rule, state and limits?

Economic Motivation answers:

How can that value or scenario be explained to help the advisor without pressure or manipulation?

Economic Motivation must consume Economic Evidence as source-bound context.

Economic Motivation must not create economic values.

Economic Motivation must not convert unknown into motivating certainty.

14. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS COMPENSATION INTELLIGENCE

Compensation Intelligence answers:

What compensation, commission, bonus, qualification, advance or retroactive state can be affirmed, estimated or projected with rules, snapshots and evidence?

Economic Motivation answers:

How can that compensation context be presented responsibly without promising money or pressuring action?

Economic Motivation must preserve paid, earned, estimated, projected, provisional, stale, conflicting and unknown compensation states.

Compensation scenario is not motivation permission.

15. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS FORECAST

Forecast answers:

What could happen?

Economic Motivation may explain forecast implications only as scenario.

Forecast does not become income.

Forecast does not become urgency.

Projected money must remain projected.

16. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS BUSINESS PLANNING

Business Planning creates action paths.

Economic Motivation provides economic context that may explain why a route matters.

Economic Motivation does not create the plan.

Business Planning must not use economic motivation as pressure.

An economic gap may inform a plan.

It must not turn a plan into mandate.

17. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS NBA

NBA prioritizes valid action candidates.

Economic Motivation provides economic context.

Economic Motivation must not convert NBA into order.

NBA must not prioritize payout over Client First.

Money may be one contextual factor.

Money is not sufficient reason for priority.

18. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS NASH

NASH produces conversational guidance and drafts.

Economic Motivation provides internal economic context.

Economic Motivation must not generate or authorize scripts that pressure clients based on advisor economic needs.

NASH must not turn advisor income gaps into client-facing manipulation.

19. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS MANAGER INTELLIGENCE

Manager Intelligence supports coaching.

Economic Motivation supports responsible advisor economic clarity.

Economic Motivation must not become pressure loop from manager to advisor to client.

Manager context may suggest review.

It must not become economic threat.

20. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE

Relationship Intelligence identifies relationship opportunity and timing.

Economic Motivation provides advisor economic context.

Economic Motivation must not convert relationships into inventory for closing income gaps.

Relationship trust is not economic leverage.

21. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION VS ADVISOR EXPERIENCE

Advisor Experience explains, teaches and reduces friction.

Economic Motivation provides economic context with Client First.

Advisor Experience may present Economic Motivation in clear language.

It must not hide uncertainty, create fear UX or make money feel more certain than it is.

22. MONEY AS CONTEXT, NOT PRESSURE

Money can explain business context.

Money can clarify tradeoffs.

Money can reveal planning constraints.

Money can help the advisor understand sustainability.

Money cannot become:

- client pressure
- product recommendation
- sales mandate
- manager enforcement
- shame prompt
- fear prompt
- hidden steering
- substitute for suitability

Economic Motivation must keep money in context.

It must not turn money into pressure.

23. PAYOUT-FIRST PROHIBITION

Forge must not recommend, prioritize, explain or frame actions primarily because payout is higher.

Payout-first behavior is prohibited when it conflicts with:

- Client First
- Product Truth
- Policy Truth
- suitability
- relationship trust
- authority boundary
- evidence limits
- uncertainty

Compensation may be visible as context.

It cannot be sufficient reason to act.

24. PARTIAL EVIDENCE HANDLING

With partial evidence, Economic Motivation may:

- explain limits
- show unknown
- suggest validation
- suggest human review
- show provisional scenario
- block strong motivation
- remind Client First
- mark uncertainty warning
- mark motivation blocked by missing evidence
- produce no economic motivation possible

With partial evidence, Economic Motivation must not:

- fake certainty
- hide uncertainty
- promise income
- pressure action
- convert scenario into mandate
- use money to manipulate
- create false urgency
- pressure client
- invent income
- invent bonus
- invent commission

25. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION STALENESS

Economic Motivation becomes stale when:

- Economic Evidence changed
- compensation scenario changed
- Forecast changed
- Business Planning changed
- income goal changed
- advisor context changed
- manager context changed
- campaign goal changed
- Policy Truth changed
- Product Truth changed
- Productivity changed
- Relationship Intelligence context changed
- rule pack changed
- snapshot changed
- new uncertainty appeared
- economic evidence became stale

Stale Economic Motivation must be marked stale.

Stale motivation may support review.

It must not support pressure.

26. ECONOMIC MOTIVATION DRIFT HANDLING

Economic Motivation Drift occurs when:

- income goal becomes client pressure
- compensation scenario becomes promise
- economic gap becomes commercial mandate
- productivity becomes economic shame
- manager context becomes hierarchical pressure
- relationship context becomes exploitation
- NBA becomes order because of money
- NASH becomes manipulative script
- payout becomes recommendation reason
- Client First becomes subordinate to advisor income
- uncertainty is hidden for motivation
- projected money is presented as real money

When drift exists, Forge must:

- mark payout-first risk
- mark pressure risk
- restore uncertainty
- restore Client First
- restore source boundaries
- restore Authority Boundary
- block pressure language
- block shame language
- block client manipulation
- require validation or human review
- preserve unknown

27. CLIENT FIRST

Client First is the controlling boundary for Economic Motivation.

Advisor income does not justify unsuitable advice.

Bonus opportunity does not justify product push.

Commission does not justify client pressure.

Economic gap does not justify manipulation.

Campaign goal does not justify violating client trust.

Relationship opportunity does not justify exploiting client context.

Economic Motivation is valid only when it preserves client suitability, autonomy, trust and truth.

28. AUTHORITY BOUNDARY

Economic Motivation may inform.

Economic Motivation may explain.

Economic Motivation may contextualize.

Economic Motivation may suggest review.

Economic Motivation may not decide.

Economic Motivation may not mandate.

Economic Motivation may not execute.

Economic Motivation may not replace advisor, manager or client authority.

Economic Motivation may not promise money.

Economic Motivation may not create enforcement.

Human authority remains governed by ADR-003.

29. SURVIVAL TEST

If ADR-018 disappears:

- Economic Motivation can become anxiety-driven sales
- Advisor Experience can become fear-based UX
- NASH can become client pressure script
- NBA can become payout-first order
- Business Planning can become money-pressure checklist
- Manager Intelligence can create pressure loop
- Relationship Intelligence can become exploitation of network
- Compensation Intelligence can be used as promise
- Economic Evidence can be used as motivation without limits
- Client First can be subordinated to advisor income
- Authority Boundary can be weakened by financial pressure

ADR-018 survives because no prior ADR fully governs how economic context is presented motivationally without pressure, shame, anxiety or manipulation.

30. ABSORPTION TEST

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-008.

ADR-008 governs economic evidence, not motivational presentation.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-017.

ADR-017 governs compensation evidence, not motivation or pressure.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-012.

ADR-012 governs planning, not economic motivation posture.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-009.

ADR-009 governs priority, not motivation.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-010.

ADR-010 governs conversation language, not advisor economic motivation.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-015.

ADR-015 governs manager coaching, not advisor economic motivation.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-016.

ADR-016 governs advisor experience and learning, not economic motivation truth.

ADR-018 cannot be absorbed into ADR-011.

ADR-011 governs relationship non-manipulation, not economic motivation.

ADR-018 must survive as independent boundary.

31. ALLOWED BEHAVIOR

Economic Motivation may:

- explain economic scenarios with limits
- show economic gaps as context
- connect income goals with responsible plans
- explain activity/income tradeoffs without shame
- explain estimated compensation without promising payment
- show tradeoffs without inducing pressure
- help prioritize review of opportunities without payout-first logic
- suggest validation of economic evidence
- suggest human review when scenario is sensitive
- help advisor understand business impact
- reinforce Client First
- reduce anxiety by explaining uncertainty
- block economic motivation when evidence is missing
- produce no economic motivation possible

32. DISALLOWED BEHAVIOR

Economic Motivation must not:

- pressure advisor with money
- pressure client for advisor money
- recommend product by commission
- prioritize payout over suitability
- convert income gap into mandate
- convert compensation scenario into promise
- convert forecast into real income
- use economic fear
- use economic shame
- use Manager Intelligence for pressure
- use Productivity for shame
- use Relationship Intelligence to exploit network
- use NASH for manipulation
- use NBA as order
- hide uncertainty for motivation
- invent income
- invent bonuses
- invent commissions
- present money as sufficient reason for commercial recommendation
- create motivation score
- create behavior model
- create urgency logic

33. CONSEQUENCES

Positive consequences:

- economic context becomes safer
- Client First is protected
- payout-first behavior decreases
- advisor anxiety decreases
- Business Planning becomes more responsible
- NASH avoids economic pressure scripts
- NBA avoids payout distortion
- Manager Intelligence avoids pressure loops
- Relationship Intelligence avoids exploitation
- Advisor Experience can explain money without false certainty
- Compensation Intelligence can be consumed without becoming promise

Tradeoffs:

- less aggressive motivation language
- more visible uncertainty
- more blocked motivational outputs
- more human review for sensitive scenarios
- less ability to use money as urgency
- more friction when economic evidence is missing
- fewer payout-first shortcuts

These tradeoffs are acceptable because economic motivation that violates Client First is not intelligence.

37. FINAL VERDICT

ADR-018 establishes that Forge may use economic context to help the advisor understand goals, gaps, scenarios and tradeoffs.

Forge may not use money as pressure.

Forge may not use compensation as product recommendation.

Forge may not use income gaps to pressure clients.

Forge may not hide uncertainty to motivate action.

Money is context, not pressure.

Payout is not suitability.

Economic gap is not mandate.

Compensation scenario is not promise.

Forecast money is not real money.

Client First remains above advisor income.

Economic Motivation is valid only when it preserves evidence, uncertainty, human authority and Client First.

Economic motivation that causes client pressure, advisor shame, payout-first behavior or hidden steering is not Forge intelligence.

It is financial anxiety with a product mask.
