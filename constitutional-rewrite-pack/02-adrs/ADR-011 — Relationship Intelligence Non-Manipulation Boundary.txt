ADR-011 — RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE NON-MANIPULATION BOUNDARY

1. TITLE

ADR-011 — Relationship Intelligence Non-Manipulation Boundary

2. STATUS

Final

3. CONTEXT

Forge opera sobre señales relacionales: timing, engagement, confianza, historial, oportunidades de referral, eventos de vida, cercanía, reciprocidad, conversaciones previas y contexto humano.

Relationship Intelligence existe para responder:

¿Qué relación, oportunidad, riesgo o momento relacional puede ayudar al asesor a actuar con más cuidado, relevancia y confianza?

Relationship Intelligence no responde:

- qué cliente debe ser presionado
- qué evento de vida debe ser explotado
- qué relación pertenece al manager
- qué confianza puede usarse como palanca
- qué mensaje final debe enviarse
- qué intención no confirmada tiene el cliente
- qué venta debe ocurrir por cercanía relacional

Sin Relationship Intelligence Non-Manipulation Boundary, Forge puede:

- manipular cliente o asesor
- convertir señales relacionales en presión comercial
- usar eventos de vida como trigger de venta
- explotar confianza como palanca
- confundir oportunidad relacional con recomendación o permiso
- convertir relación en inventario comercial
- mezclar inputs de NASH, NBA, Advisor Experience, Forecast y Economic Evidence sin límites
- inventar intención del cliente
- generar mensajes finales sin autoridad humana
- dañar Client First

ADR-011 protege la frontera entre inteligencia relacional y manipulación.

4. DECISION

Relationship Intelligence es el owner conceptual de señales relacionales.

Puede identificar oportunidades relacionales, engagement, riesgo de manipulación, posibles referrals, timing relacional y contexto humano relevante.

Relationship Intelligence no puede generar mensajes finales, presionar al cliente, ejecutar contacto, inferir consentimiento ni redefinir Product Truth o Policy Truth.

Las decisiones finales corresponden al asesor humano y deben respetar Authority Boundary y Client First.

Relación no es permiso.

Confianza no es palanca.

Evento de vida no es trigger automático de venta.

5. DEFINITIONS

Relationship Intelligence

Dominio que analiza señales relacionales sin ejecutar contacto ni reemplazar criterio humano.

Relationship Signal

Indicador de oportunidad, riesgo, timing, engagement o contexto relacional.

Relationship Opportunity

Posible acción relacional que requiere revisión humana y límites éticos.

Engagement Signal

Señal observable de interacción, cercanía, frecuencia, respuesta o continuidad.

Trust Boundary

Límite que impide usar confianza como palanca de presión.

Life Event Boundary

Límite que impide convertir eventos de vida en triggers automáticos de venta.

Referral Ethics

Reglas para tratar referidos con consentimiento, respeto, contexto y no manipulación.

Non-Manipulation

Principio que prohíbe presionar, explotar vulnerabilidad o convertir contexto humano en ventaja indebida.

Relationship Drift

Deterioro cuando una señal relacional se convierte en presión, explotación, manipulación o mandato comercial.

Relationship Staleness

Pérdida de vigencia de señal relacional por cambio de contexto, tiempo, interacción, confianza o evidencia.

Relationship Unknown

Estado donde existe hipótesis relacional sin confirmación suficiente.

Advisor Consent

Autorización final del asesor para decidir si actúa, cómo actúa y cuándo actúa.

Client Consent

Permiso explícito o contexto válido para contacto o uso de información relacional.

No Relationship Conclusion Possible

Estado donde la evidencia no permite una conclusión relacional responsable.

6. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE OWNERSHIP RULES

Relationship Intelligence may own:

- relationship signals
- engagement interpretation
- relationship opportunity
- referral opportunity context
- trust risk warning
- life event boundary warning
- relationship staleness warning
- relationship drift warning
- no relationship conclusion possible
- advisor review recommended
- client consent needed
- relational timing context

Relationship Intelligence does not own:

- Product Truth
- Policy Truth
- Forecast Truth
- Economic Evidence
- NBA priority truth
- NASH final message
- Business Planning truth
- Manager Intelligence conclusion
- compensation truth
- client intent
- client consent
- final recommendation
- contact execution
- sales mandate

Relationship Intelligence may inform other domains.

It must not become the authority for final action.

7. VALID RELATIONAL INPUTS

Valid relational inputs include:

- engagement histórico
- interacción reciente
- historial de referidos
- alertas de oportunidad
- contexto relacional
- eventos confirmados relevantes
- señales de comportamiento observadas
- advisor-confirmed context
- client-confirmed context
- CRM records with source
- meeting history
- follow-up history
- referral consent evidence
- relationship timeline
- life events with evidence and sensitivity limits

Each input must preserve:

- source
- owner
- period
- context
- freshness
- uncertainty
- consent status where relevant
- evidence state

8. INVALID OR WEAK RELATIONAL INPUTS

Invalid or weak inputs include:

- eventos inferidos sin evidencia
- opinión de asesor sin documentación
- presión de manager como input
- evento de vida usado como trigger de venta
- contacto sin consentimiento del asesor
- client silence treated as permission
- señales mezcladas de distintos dominios
- stale relationship data
- relationship rumor
- private inference without source
- economic pressure disguised as relationship need
- referral without permission
- trust treated as commercial leverage

9. RELATIONSHIP TRUTH HIERARCHY

Relationship Intelligence must respect this hierarchy:

Level 1: client-confirmed relational facts and consent
Level 2: advisor-confirmed relational context
Level 3: observed engagement and documented interactions
Level 4: reviewed relationship opportunities
Level 5: inferred signals with validation pending
Level 6: hypothesis without confirmation

Rules:

- confirmed relationship context beats inference
- consent beats opportunity
- Client First beats commercial urgency
- life events require sensitivity
- trust cannot become pressure
- unknown relationship intent remains unknown

10. INPUT BOUNDARIES

Relationship Intelligence must not treat proximity as permission.

Relationship Intelligence must not treat silence as consent.

Relationship Intelligence must not treat life event as sales trigger.

Relationship Intelligence must not treat referral history as open inventory.

Relationship Intelligence must not use manager pressure as relationship truth.

Relationship Intelligence must not infer client intent from weak engagement.

Relationship Intelligence must not turn economic need into relational urgency.

Relationship Intelligence must preserve Product Truth and Policy Truth boundaries.

Unknown values remain unknown.

Partial relational evidence remains partial.

11. OUTPUTS PERMITIDOS

Relationship Intelligence may output:

- relationship opportunity
- engagement signal
- referral opportunity context
- timing context
- advisor review recommended
- client consent needed
- trust boundary warning
- life event boundary warning
- manipulation risk warning
- stale relationship signal
- no relationship conclusion possible
- relational context summary
- relationship follow-up candidate
- relationship evidence gap
- uncertainty warning
- validation needed

Allowed examples:

- Existe una oportunidad relacional posible; revisar contexto antes de contactar.
- El engagement reciente sugiere timing favorable, pero no confirma intención.
- Hay evento de vida sensible; evitar presión comercial.
- Referral opportunity requiere confirmar consentimiento.
- No hay conclusión relacional responsable con la evidencia actual.

12. OUTPUTS PROHIBIDOS

Relationship Intelligence must not output:

- final client message
- contact execution
- sales mandate
- pressure instruction
- client intent certainty without evidence
- product recommendation
- policy recommendation
- economic pressure
- manipulation script
- trust leverage instruction
- life event sales trigger
- manager inventory claim
- automatic referral action
- final suitability claim
- NBA priority replacement

Prohibited examples:

- Usa su confianza para cerrar.
- Aprovecha el evento de vida para vender.
- Este cliente debe comprar.
- La relación garantiza apertura.
- Contacta automáticamente al referido.
- El manager debe presionar esta relación.

13. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE STATES

Relationship Intelligence may produce these states:

- Relationship Opportunity Available
- Advisor Review Recommended
- Client Consent Needed
- Trust Boundary Warning
- Life Event Boundary Warning
- Manipulation Risk
- Relationship Evidence Gap
- Stale Relationship Signal
- Conflicting Relationship Signals
- Observed but Limited
- No Relationship Conclusion Possible

These states describe relationship evidence and boundary posture.

They do not create permission, mandate, recommendation validity or client intent.

14. RELATIONSHIP STALENESS

Relationship signals become stale when:

- time passes without updated interaction
- client context changes
- advisor context changes
- trust context changes
- life event context changes
- relationship status changes
- consent status changes
- referral context changes
- engagement pattern changes
- CRM data is incomplete
- source evidence is superseded
- signals conflict

Stale relationship signal must not be used as strong basis without warning.

Stale signal may support review.

Stale signal must not support pressure.

15. RELATIONSHIP DRIFT HANDLING

Relationship Drift occurs when:

- relationship opportunity becomes sales mandate
- trust becomes leverage
- life event becomes trigger
- referral becomes inventory
- engagement becomes client intent
- manager pressure becomes relationship truth
- NASH language becomes manipulation
- NBA priority becomes contact order
- economic motivation becomes pressure
- relationship context becomes surveillance
- unknown becomes permission

When drift exists, Forge must:

- mark manipulation risk
- restore consent boundary
- restore Client First
- restore Authority Boundary
- mark uncertainty
- separate relationship signal from recommendation
- block automatic contact
- prevent pressure language
- preserve unknown

16. PARTIAL EVIDENCE HANDLING

With partial relational evidence, Relationship Intelligence may:

- mark observed but limited
- mark advisor review recommended
- mark client consent needed
- identify missing evidence
- declare uncertainty
- show possible relationship opportunity
- show no relationship conclusion possible
- request validation

With partial relational evidence, Relationship Intelligence must not:

- infer client intent
- infer consent
- generate final message
- recommend sale
- trigger contact
- treat opportunity as permission
- hide uncertainty
- convert unknown into action

17. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS NASH

NASH owns conversation intelligence and language drafts.

Relationship Intelligence may provide relational context.

NASH must not use relationship context to manipulate, pressure or exploit vulnerability.

Relationship Intelligence cannot generate final NASH message.

NASH may draft.

Advisor decides.

18. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS NBA

NBA owns action prioritization.

Relationship Intelligence may provide relationship opportunity as candidate context.

Relationship opportunity is not global priority by itself.

NBA must preserve ADR-004, ADR-009, Authority Boundary and Client First.

Relationship Intelligence cannot convert opportunity into mandate.

19. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS BUSINESS PLANNING

Business Planning may use relationship opportunity as planning input.

Relationship opportunity must not become obligation.

Plans must not exploit client trust, life events or referral relationships.

20. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS ADVISOR EXPERIENCE

Advisor Experience may teach responsible relationship handling.

Advisor Experience must not turn relationship signals into dependency, hidden pressure or fake certainty.

Relationship Intelligence may provide context.

Advisor Experience may explain boundaries.

21. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS MANAGER INTELLIGENCE

Manager Intelligence may observe relationship context for coaching.

Manager Intelligence must not convert relationships into commercial inventory or hierarchical pressure.

Manager attention does not create client permission.

22. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS FORECAST / ECONOMIC MOTIVATION

Forecast may use engagement as weak contextual input only with limits.

Economic Motivation must not use relationship context to create income pressure.

Relationship trust cannot be converted into economic urgency.

Projected income does not justify relational pressure.

23. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE VS PRODUCT / POLICY TRUTH

Relationship Intelligence cannot redefine Product Truth.

Relationship Intelligence cannot redefine Policy Truth.

Relationship proximity does not prove suitability.

Client trust does not validate coverage, premiums, benefits or policy status.

24. AUTHORITY BOUNDARY

Relationship Intelligence may inform.

Relationship Intelligence may warn.

Relationship Intelligence may suggest review.

Relationship Intelligence may not decide.

Relationship Intelligence may not execute contact.

Relationship Intelligence may not infer consent.

Relationship Intelligence may not replace advisor, manager or client authority.

Advisor decides whether and how to act.

Client retains autonomy and consent.

25. CLIENT FIRST

Relationship Intelligence must preserve Client First.

Client trust, autonomy, suitability and context are more important than conversion pressure.

Relationship Intelligence must not:

- exploit vulnerability
- exploit life events
- pressure referrals
- hide commercial intent
- use friendship as leverage
- use family context as leverage
- use economic gap as pressure
- use manager pressure as reason to contact

If a relationship opportunity harms client trust, it is invalid.

26. SURVIVAL TEST

If ADR-011 disappears:

- relationship opportunity can become manipulation
- trust can become sales leverage
- life events can become triggers
- referrals can become inventory
- engagement can become fake intent
- NASH can become pressure language
- NBA can become contact mandate
- Manager Intelligence can pressure relationship exploitation
- Economic Motivation can exploit relationship trust
- Client First can be harmed

ADR-011 survives because no prior ADR fully governs non-manipulation of relationship signals.

27. ABSORPTION TEST

ADR-011 cannot be fully absorbed into ADR-003.

ADR-003 governs authority, but not relationship-specific manipulation risk.

ADR-011 cannot be fully absorbed into ADR-004.

ADR-004 governs recommendation validity, but not trust, consent, referral ethics or life event boundary.

ADR-011 cannot be fully absorbed into ADR-009.

ADR-009 governs action priority, but not relational non-manipulation.

ADR-011 cannot be fully absorbed into ADR-010.

NASH governs conversation language, but not relationship truth ownership.

ADR-011 cannot be fully absorbed into ADR-015 or ADR-016.

Manager Intelligence and Advisor Experience consume relationship context, but do not own relational ethics.

ADR-011 remains an independent constitutional boundary.

28. ALLOWED BEHAVIOR

Relationship Intelligence may:

- identify opportunity relacional
- detect engagement bajo or alto
- identify referral context
- signal trust boundary risk
- signal life event sensitivity
- suggest advisor review before contact
- mark confidence and uncertainty
- mark stale relational signal
- mark consent needed
- contextualize relationship history
- preserve Client First
- preserve Authority Boundary
- return no relationship conclusion possible

29. DISALLOWED BEHAVIOR

Relationship Intelligence must not:

- invent client intent
- infer consent
- use trust as leverage
- transform life event into sales trigger
- generate final message
- execute contact
- redefine Product Truth
- redefine Policy Truth
- prioritize globally as NBA
- replace human authority
- create pressure language
- convert relationships into manager inventory
- exploit vulnerability
- hide uncertainty
- hide consent limits

30. CONSEQUENCES

Positive consequences:

- relationships are protected
- ethical opportunities are detected
- NASH becomes safer
- Advisor Experience becomes safer
- Manager Intelligence becomes safer
- manipulation risk decreases
- client trust is preserved
- referral ethics are clearer
- relationship timing becomes useful without becoming pressure

Tradeoffs:

- more input validation
- more advisor review
- less automation of contact
- more limits on relationship-driven alerts
- some opportunities remain unacted until consent or context is clear
- relationship intelligence may feel less aggressive commercially

34. FINAL VERDICT

Relationship Intelligence may detect opportunity, engagement, timing, referral context and relational risk.

Relationship Intelligence may not manipulate.

Relationship Intelligence may not pressure the client.

Relationship Intelligence may not generate final messages.

Relationship Intelligence may not redefine Product Truth or Policy Truth.

Relationship opportunity is not permission.

Trust is not leverage.

Life event is not sales trigger.

Referral is not inventory.

Engagement is not client intent.

The final decision always depends on human advisor authority and Client First.

An opportunity without consent, evidence, uncertainty and non-manipulation boundary is not Relationship Intelligence.

It is commercial pressure disguised as context.
