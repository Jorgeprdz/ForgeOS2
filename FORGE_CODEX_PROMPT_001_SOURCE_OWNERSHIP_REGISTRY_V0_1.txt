PRIMARY IMPLEMENTATION GOAL:

Create a minimal, isolated Source Ownership Registry v0.1 that can answer:

- Who owns this source type?
- Who owns this claim type?
- Which ADR governs the ownership?
- Which domains may consume it?
- Which reinterpretations are prohibited?
- Is the owner known, unknown, conflicting or not applicable?
- Should strong output be blocked because ownership is unknown or conflicting?

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR:

The registry must preserve the principle:

Ownership is not validity.

The registry does not validate evidence.

The registry does not decide output strength.

The registry does not recommend actions.

The registry does not execute workflows.

The registry does not infer business truth.

The registry only identifies conceptual ownership and consumption boundaries.

- owner is unknown
- owner is conflicting
- consuming domain is restricted

- claim attempts to convert relationship context into permission

CRITICAL OWNERSHIP REQUIREMENTS:

UDI / FX:

- current_udi_value owner must be Economic Evidence.
- fx_rate owner must be Economic Evidence.
- Product Truth may consume only for product currency semantics.
- Forecast may consume only for projections with explicit assumptions.
- Product Truth must not own UDI/FX source truth.

Quote / illustration:

- raw_quote_illustration_document owner must be Evidence Ownership.
- quoted values owner must be Economic Evidence.
- projection derived from quote owner must be Forecast Truth.
- quote must not be treated as Policy Truth.
- quote must not be treated as payment.

NASH:

- NASH owns conversation guidance, message framing, tone options and language suggestions.
- NASH must not own client intent truth.
- NASH must not own relationship permission.
- NASH must not own product truth.
- NASH must not own policy truth.

Relationship Intelligence:

- owns relationship context, timing signals and opportunity signals.
- does not own permission to contact.
- does not own permission to manipulate.
- does not own client intent.
- does not own commercial obligation.

Mick / Productivity / raw activity:

- raw_system_activity_log owner must be Evidence Ownership.
- productivity_metric owner must be Productivity.
- mick_behavior_signal owner must be Mick Behavior Intelligence.
- Mick must not own raw logs.
- Productivity must not own human worth.
- Manager Intelligence must not convert either into enforcement.

Policy / premium:

- paid_premium_record owner must be Policy Truth.
- issued_premium_record owner must be Policy Truth.
- issued premium is not paid premium.
- paid premium requires payment evidence.
- compensation cannot redefine paid premium.

Compensation:

- compensation_rule_pack owner must be Compensation Intelligence.
- estimated commission is not paid income.
- bonus scenario is not confirmed bonus.
- paid income requires payment evidence.

Economic Motivation:

- owns economic presentation posture / framing.
- does not own economic truth.
- does not own compensation truth.
- must not create pressure against Client First.
