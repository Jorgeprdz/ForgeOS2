FORGE BENVENÙ LEOPARD EXPERIENCE COPY DIRECTIONS

Status:
COPY DIRECTION / INTERNAL PRODUCT SPEC / NOT IMPLEMENTED

Tone:
- Human
- Sober
- Premium
- Emotional
- Clear
- Not cheesy

Avoid:
- Corporate onboarding language
- Cheap coaching language
- Conference motivation
- Impossible promises
- Long phrases
- Feature selling
- Dashboard language
- CRM language
- External brand references

Internal rule:
"Leopard Experience" is an internal concept name only. Do not expose that name
to the advisor-facing experience.

VERSION 1 - QUIET CAREER SYSTEM

Act 1 - Darkness / Silence:
No viniste a llenar otro CRM.

Act 2 - Reveal:
Viniste a construir una carrera que funcione.

Act 3 - Intelligence Awakens:
Cada conversación, cada póliza y cada seguimiento dejan señales.

Act 4 - Promise:
Forge las conecta para ayudarte a decidir qué hacer después.

Act 5 - Transition:
Antes de empezar, veamos hacia dónde quieres ir.

Direction:
This version is the cleanest and most sober. It is best when the visual system
already carries emotion and the copy should not over-explain.

VERSION 2 - FUTURE CAREER

Act 1 - Darkness / Silence:
Esto no empieza con un formulario.

Act 2 - Reveal:
Empieza con la carrera que estás construyendo.

Act 3 - Intelligence Awakens:
Lo que haces hoy puede convertirse en dirección, oportunidad y seguimiento.

Act 4 - Promise:
Forge no viene a darte más trabajo. Viene a ayudarte a ver el siguiente paso.

Act 5 - Transition:
Miremos primero el futuro que quieres construir.

Direction:
This version is warmer and more explicit. Use it if testing shows advisors need
more clarity about why the experience exists.

VERSION 3 - HUMAN COMPANION

Act 1 - Darkness / Silence:
Antes de datos, hay una historia.

Act 2 - Reveal:
La tuya: clientes, decisiones, hábitos, oportunidades y futuro.

Act 3 - Intelligence Awakens:
Forge aprende a conectar esas señales para acompañarte mejor.

Act 4 - Promise:
No te voy a entrevistar. Voy a entender contigo.

Act 5 - Transition:
Empecemos por lo que quieres construir.

Direction:
This version is the most human and emotional. Use carefully; it needs restrained
visuals so it does not become sentimental.

COPY QUALITY RULES

PASS:
- The sentence can be understood on first read.
- The line sounds like Forge, not a corporation.
- The copy invites reflection without pressure.
- The experience can move forward without asking for data.
- The line supports Future You Conversation.

FAIL:
- The copy says "complete your profile."
- The copy asks for metrics too early.
- The copy sounds like a SaaS landing page.
- The copy sounds like motivational speaking.
- The copy promises success, income or transformation.
- The copy explains features before meaning.

RECOMMENDED STARTING DIRECTION

Start with Version 1.

Use Version 2 if the prototype needs more explicit clarity.

Use Version 3 only if the visual language is restrained and the experience does
not become sentimental.
