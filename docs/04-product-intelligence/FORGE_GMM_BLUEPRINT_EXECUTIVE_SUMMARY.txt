5. EXAMPLE QUESTION

User:

"My wife has had Alfa Medical for 4 years.
She is 33.
She is pregnant.
Would childbirth be covered?"

Blueprint response path:

- Intake Framing detects case-specific coverage question.
- Product Router routes to Alfa Medical, pending caratula confirmation.
- Event Family Router classifies as Maternity.
- Insured Context sees age 33 as consistent with basic maternity age range.
- Waiting/continuity path notes 4 years appears to exceed 10-month continuity
  requirement if continuous through birth.
- Human Review Gate remains open because expected birth date, caratula,
  active policy, territory, deductible type, assisted reproduction status and
  documentation are missing.
- Assessment Language Selector chooses Conditional Assessment.

Proper answer:

Conditionally, this routes to Alfa Medical maternity year 4+ rules and may be
eligible if coverage is continuous, the policy is active, the birth occurs in
the covered territory, deductible type and caratula values support the benefit,
assisted reproduction rules do not change the path, and required documentation
is provided. Forge must not say simply "yes, covered."
