## 2. What Protection Is Being Proposed?

The proposal is for Alfa Medical, a major medical expense protection plan.

In plain language:

This is protection for the kind of medical event that can become bigger than a
normal savings account: an accident, a serious illness, hospitalization,
surgery, emergency treatment or a covered medical situation that requires
professional care.

The proposal is not mainly about paying for small doctor visits.

It is about protecting Lariza from a medical event becoming a financial crisis.

## 6. Forge Sales Reading

This quote is strong because it gives Lariza broad protection while she is young
and likely insurable at normal risk.

The key conversation is not:

"Do you like this insurance?"

The key conversation is:

"If something serious happened to your health this year, would you rather face
the medical bill alone or transfer most of that risk while you are young and
healthy enough to qualify?"
