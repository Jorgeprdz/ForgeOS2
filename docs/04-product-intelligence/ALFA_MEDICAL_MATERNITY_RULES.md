# Alfa Medical Maternity Rules

Status: DISCOVERY ONLY

## AM-MAT-001

Description: Basic maternity applies only to insured women within age range.

Required Inputs:
- Mother insured status.
- Mother age at pregnancy.
- Pregnancy evidence.

Conditions:
- Woman must become pregnant between ages 15 and 44.

Exceptions:
- Reproduction-assisted maternity has a separate age range.

Unknowns:
- How age is evaluated for pregnancies crossing birthday boundaries.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.

## AM-MAT-002

Description: Basic maternity requires 10 months of continuous coverage at
birth.

Required Inputs:
- Coverage start.
- Prior eligible SMNYL GMM coverage.
- Birth date.

Conditions:
- At birth, mother must have at least 10 months continuous coverage under this
  policy or qualifying SMNYL GMM individual/family policy.

Exceptions:
- Waiting-period elimination/reduction does not apply.

Unknowns:
- Documents required to prove prior qualifying coverage.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.

## AM-MAT-003

Description: Maternity years 1 to 3 indemnifies up to maternity benefit sum.

Required Inputs:
- Policy year.
- Maternity benefit sum insured.
- Birth certificate or prenatal ultrasound if advance payment.
- Clinical history.

Conditions:
- Year 1 to 3 uninterrupted policy.
- Indemnity by maternity benefit sum at birth or eligible advance payment.
- Territory, coinsurance, plan and deductible are those contracted 10 months
  before birth.
- No deductible or coinsurance applies to this maternity benefit.

Exceptions:
- Advance payment cannot be paid again at birth.
- Deducible en Exceso exclusion may apply.

Unknowns:
- Eligible deductibles from public portal.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.A.1.

## AM-MAT-004

Description: Maternity year 4 onward covers defined prenatal, birth and
postnatal expenses.

Required Inputs:
- Policy year.
- Birth evidence.
- Caratula values.
- Covered expenses.

Conditions:
- From fourth year of continuous coverage.
- Covers defined prenatal consultations, birth/cesarean services, newborn
  routine care and limited postnatal consultations.
- Deductible and coinsurance apply.

Exceptions:
- No coinsurance reduction under referenced coinsurance clause.

Unknowns:
- Whether client elects year 1-3 benefit behavior after year 4.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.A.2.

## AM-MAT-005

Description: Maternity benefit does not apply with Deducible en Exceso.

Required Inputs:
- Deductible type.
- Maternity benefit claim.

Conditions:
- If contracted deductible is Deducible en Exceso, maternity benefit is not
  applicable.

Exceptions:
- None identified.

Unknowns:
- Whether optional endorsement can modify.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.A exclusion.

## AM-MAT-006

Description: Pregnancy complications are covered under listed and open-ended
examples after 10 months continuous coverage.

Required Inputs:
- Complication diagnosis.
- Coverage continuity.
- Caratula values at claim.

Conditions:
- Mother must have at least 10 months continuous coverage at time of claim.
- Covered examples include molar pregnancy, extrauterine pregnancy,
  preeclampsia, eclampsia, placenta accreta, placenta previa, uterine atony,
  puerperal sepsis, non-voluntary abortion or curettage.

Exceptions:
- Punishable/voluntary abortions and related complications.
- Omitted notice of assisted reproduction where applicable.
- If birth occurs during complication treatment, maternity benefit is not later
  granted.

Unknowns:
- Classification of complications not listed.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.B.

## AM-MAT-007

Description: Newborn coverage applies only under specific biological,
gestational and inclusion rules.

Required Inputs:
- Biological/gestational relationship.
- Mother coverage continuity.
- Whether maternity was covered.
- Inclusion request date.

Conditions:
- Child must be biological and gestated by insured mother.
- Mother must have 10 months continuous coverage.
- Maternity must have been covered.
- Inclusion request must occur within 30 days for no medical selection.

Exceptions:
- Late inclusion triggers medical selection.

Unknowns:
- Premium handling and continuation details.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.C.

## AM-MAT-008

Description: Reproduction-assisted maternity has separate eligibility.

Required Inputs:
- Mother age.
- Infertility/sterility diagnosis or homoparental family status.
- Method used.
- Embryo count.
- Institution approval.
- Coverage continuity.

Conditions:
- Woman must become pregnant between ages 20 and 35.
- Method must be IVF or artificial insemination.
- Maximum two embryos transferred.
- Medical institution must have health authority approval or similar abroad.
- Required insured persons must have at least 10 months continuous coverage.

Exceptions:
- Prior fertility-control treatment, prior gender-change treatment, use of
  another person's eggs and other particular exclusions.

Unknowns:
- Evidence for homoparental and partner-insured requirements.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.2.

## AM-MAT-009

Description: Assisted reproduction notice is mandatory.

Required Inputs:
- Written notice.
- First trimester timing.
- Forms used.

Conditions:
- Mother must notify insurer in writing about fertility treatment during first
  trimester through current forms.

Exceptions:
- Omission excludes related newborn and mother expenses under particular
  exclusions.

Unknowns:
- Current forms and submission channel.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.2.A; IV.2 exclusions.

## AM-MAT-010

Description: Fetal surgery does not apply when maternity is by assisted
reproduction.

Required Inputs:
- Pregnancy origin.
- Requested fetal surgery.

Conditions:
- Fetal surgery benefit excludes reproduction-assisted maternity.

Exceptions:
- None identified.

Unknowns:
- Whether separate endorsement can alter.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.18 exclusions.
