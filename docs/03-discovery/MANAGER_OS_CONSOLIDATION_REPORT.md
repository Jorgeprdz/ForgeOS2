## 3. MANAGER OS GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

**VERDICT:** **Governance Domains under a single Manager Governance Authority.**

**Reasoning:**
While each domain (Allocation, Health, Leadership) protects a unique decision, creating three new Council Seats would lead to **Governance Inflation**. 
- **Andrey (Seat #13)** shall act as the primary constitutional representative for the **Manager OS Layer**.
- Organization Health and Leadership Intelligence shall operate as **Specialized Domains** under the authority of **The Allocator**.
- This maintains the Council size at 13 (an odd number for voting) while ensuring all managerial decisions are represented.

## 4. KEY DISCOVERIES (FORMAL DOCUMENTATION)

### DISCOVERY A: "The Advisor is not the customer. The Manager is the customer."
- **Why it matters:** Prevents "Judgment Leakage" where investment scores (RODI) could demotivate the advisor.
- **Decision:** Establishes the "Miranda Wall" between OS layers.
- **Risk if ignored:** Organizational toxicity and loss of psychological safety for advisors.

### DISCOVERY B: "Success is not leadership. Replication is leadership."
- **Why it matters:** Prevents promoting "Unicorns" (high producers who can't teach) into management roles.
- **Decision:** Sets "Mentee RODI Delta" as the primary evidence for promotion.
- **Risk if ignored:** Collapse of the franchise model and stalled organizational growth.

### DISCOVERY C: "Andrey does not protect people. Andrey protects finite development capacity."
- **Why it matters:** Reframes selection as an economic necessity rather than a personal judgment.
- **Decision:** Authorizes Veto based on resource scarcity.
- **Risk if ignored:** Manager burnout and resource leakage on non-viable units.

### DISCOVERY D: "Organization Health protects the system. Not the individual."
- **Why it matters:** Identifies systemic decay (attrition contagion) that individual engines cannot see.
- **Decision:** Triggers systemic interventions across the entire team.
- **Risk if ignored:** Team-wide collapse despite high performance of specific "stars."
