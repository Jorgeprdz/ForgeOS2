G) Constitutional review

ADR alignment:

- ADR-001: Unknown ownership blocks strong output.
- ADR-002: Metric ownership remains separate from consumer domains.
- ADR-003: Registry does not decide, approve, execute or enforce, and dual source plus claim inputs are surfaced as explicit ambiguity.
- ADR-004: Registry blocks prohibited reinterpretation but does not create recommendations or action authorization.
- Recommendation-shaped claim ownership entries are ownership taxonomy only; they are not recommendation approval, prioritization approval, action authorization, workflow authorization or recommendation validity.
- ADR-005: Product Truth owns product rules and conditions.
- ADR-006: Policy Truth owns policy documents, policy events and premium states.
- ADR-007: Forecast Truth owns projections and assumptions, not facts.
- ADR-008: Economic Evidence owns UDI, FX and economic values.
- ADR-009: NBA owns priority, not mandate.
- ADR-010: NASH owns conversation guidance, not client intent.
- ADR-011: Relationship Intelligence owns opportunity/context, not permission.
- ADR-012: Business Planning owns goals/plans, not source truth.
- ADR-013: Mick owns behavior pattern signals, not character or motivation.
- ADR-014: Productivity owns metrics, not human worth.
- ADR-015: Manager Intelligence owns coaching context, not enforcement.
- ADR-016: Advisor Experience owns support/learning context, not truth.
- ADR-017: Compensation Intelligence owns compensation interpretation, not payment without evidence.
- ADR-018: Economic Motivation owns economic presentation posture, not economic truth or pressure.

Core boundary preserved:
Ownership is not validity.
Blocked means strong output is not constitutionally supported, not workflow gating.
Validation_required means additional validation is required elsewhere; the registry does not validate.
