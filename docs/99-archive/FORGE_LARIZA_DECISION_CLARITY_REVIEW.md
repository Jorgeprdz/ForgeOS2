# Forge Lariza Decision Clarity Review

## What Lariza Gains by Having the Policy

She gains a financial backstop for major covered medical events.

That means:

- A serious accident does not have to become a full financial burden.
- A hospitalization does not have to depend only on savings or family support.
- She has a clearer plan before something happens.
- She can make the decision while she is young and healthy.
- She creates a foundation for future health protection.

## What Lariza Risks by Not Having It

If she does not have this protection, she keeps the full financial risk.

That means:

- If a serious medical event happens, the cost comes from savings, family,
  credit or delayed treatment.
- A health crisis can become a financial crisis.
- If her health changes later, buying protection may be harder or more
  expensive.
- She may lose the chance to protect herself while she is a normal risk.

## Tradeoffs in This Quote

### Cost vs Protection

The annual premium is $47,388.27.

The question is:

"Is this amount worth paying to avoid carrying the full financial risk of a
major medical event?"

### First Responsibility vs Premium

The quote has a $25,000 first responsibility.

Plain language:

Lariza keeps the first layer of cost, and transfers the larger risk.

### National vs International

This quote is national.

If Lariza travels or spends time abroad, the advisor should discuss whether
foreign protection matters.

### Core Protection vs Optional Add-ons

The quote includes accident deductible elimination, but several other optional
coverages are only described as available.

Lariza should decide intentionally which add-ons matter.

## Why Someone Her Age Might Consider It

Because protection is not only for people who are already sick.

At 23:

- She may qualify more easily.
- She may protect her future insurability.
- She may be building independence.
- She may avoid depending on emergency family support.
- She has time to make the policy part of her long-term financial life.

## What Should Be Clarified Before Purchase

- Does Lariza understand national territoriality?
- Is Plan Integro aligned with the hospitals she would want?
- Is $25,000 comfortable as the first amount she would absorb?
- Is annual payment comfortable?
- Does she need foreign coverage?
- Does she care about Dental Premium?
- Does maternity matter to her planning?
- Does she understand that optional coverages described are not automatically
  selected?
- Does she understand the quote is illustrative until issued?

## Decision Clarity Statement

This proposal is not asking Lariza to predict illness.

It is asking her to decide whether she wants a serious medical event to be her
full financial responsibility.
