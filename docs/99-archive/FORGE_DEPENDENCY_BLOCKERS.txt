BLOCKER GROUP 6 - ANTI-SCORE / ANTI-RANKING

Truths blocked by reduction risk:

- Professional Excellence.
- Professional Development.
- Readiness.
- Purpose Snapshot use.
- Manager Intelligence visibility.

Needed protections:

- No single score for human growth.
- No public leaderboard default.
- No motivation score.
- No readiness score without context.
- No excellence ranking as default.
- No manager pressure mechanisms.

TRUTHS WITH NO MAJOR BLOCKER

These are ready for future lock review:

- Evidence must precede judgment.
- Human dignity must be preserved.
- Purpose is human context, not pressure.
- Manager Intelligence develops people, not surveils them.
- Production is evidence, not proof.
- No single score should represent human growth, excellence or readiness.
- Forge converts context into decisions and action paths.
- Forge is not a CRM, dashboard collection, database or sales tracker.
- Value before work.
- Learning by doing.
- Context before metric.
- Potential, Readiness and Excellence are distinct.
