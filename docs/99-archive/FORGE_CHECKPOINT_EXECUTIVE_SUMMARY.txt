SUMMARY

This checkpoint consolidates Professional Sales Process, Universal Sales
Principles, Readiness Intelligence, Friction Intelligence and Action Safety
discoveries.

The strongest consolidated finding is:

Forge should understand professional selling and human development decisions as
decision-readiness processes, not product-presentation processes.

UNIFIED MODEL

Awareness
-> Recognition
-> Ownership
-> Commitment
-> Decision
-> Action

Readiness indicates preparation to move.

Friction explains why movement stalls.

Action Safety explains what makes movement feel responsible enough despite
uncertainty.

Decision Clarity helps people understand what matters, what is known, what is
uncertain and what happens next.
