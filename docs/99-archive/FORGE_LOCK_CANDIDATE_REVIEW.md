## 2. Candidate 1: Product Follows Human Context

Evidence strength:

HIGH

Source convergence:

Strong convergence.

- Pedro: product categories follow risk recognition and risk transfer.
- SMNYL: ANF and priorities precede solution presentation.
- Kinder: product-centered selling is weaker than client-centered buying
  assistance.

Counterexamples:

- transactional product purchases
- repeat purchases with existing clients
- regulatory product explanation requirements
- cases where client already knows the problem and asks directly for product

Universal applicability:

High for advisory, consultative and high-trust decisions.

Lower for commodity transactions.

Candidate for future constitutional review:

YES, as a strong Forge Core candidate.

Recommended status:

READY FOR FUTURE REVIEW, NOT LOCKED

## 3. Candidate 2: Professional Selling Is the Structured Development of Human Decision Readiness

Evidence strength:

MEDIUM-HIGH

Source convergence:

Strong across Kinder and SMNYL; supported conceptually by Pedro.

Insurance-specific or universal:

Likely broader than insurance, but the evidence base reviewed is heavily
financial-services-oriented.

Relationship to Career OS:

Strong. It frames selling as developing human capability to recognize, decide
and act responsibly.

Relationship to Sales Intelligence:

Direct. Sales Intelligence should understand readiness formation, not only close
activity.

Candidate for future constitutional review:

YES, but requires real-world validation beyond source documents.

Recommended status:

CANDIDATE FOR LOCK REVIEW AFTER VALIDATION

## 4. Candidate 3: Forge Helps People Make Better Decisions

Evidence strength:

HIGH

Existing alignment:

Strong alignment with Decision Clarity First and Forge's core operating
principle that intelligence should lead to action.

Relationship to Article 0:

Strong. Article 0 proposes Forge exists to strengthen human judgment, not
replace it.

Universal applicability:

Very high. Applies across advisor, manager, client, candidate, recruit and
partner contexts.

Constitutional candidate:

YES.

Risk:

Must not become "Forge decides for people." The candidate must preserve human
agency and judgment.

Recommended status:

STRONG CONSTITUTIONAL CANDIDATE, NOT RATIFIED

## 5. Candidate 4: Action Safety Is a Valid Human Decision Concept

Evidence strength:

MEDIUM

Evidence gaps:

- no real meeting transcript validation yet
- no cross-domain validation outside financial services
- no evidence contract
- no confirmed boundary between genuine safety and social compliance

Validation requirements:

- client meeting transcripts
- advisor follow-up observations
- manager coaching sessions
- recruiting conversations
- post-decision implementation tracking

Risk of misuse:

High.

Action Safety could be misused to increase compliance, pressure, urgency
theater or emotional manipulation.

Candidate status:

PROMISING DISCOVERY CANDIDATE, NOT READY FOR LOCK

Recommended status:

NEEDS VALIDATION AND ETHICAL BOUNDARY REVIEW

## 6. Lock Priority Recommendation

Recommended future review order:

1. Forge helps people make better decisions.
2. Product follows human context.
3. Professional selling develops decision readiness.
4. Action Safety as candidate concept.

Reason:

The first two are broader, better supported and more protective. Action Safety
is powerful but riskier and less validated.
