## Scenario Definitions Stated In The PDF

- Escenario economico base (Hoy): possible retirement income assuming the rate earned on the fund is the same as today's rate during the whole savings period.
- Escenario economico favorable: possible retirement income assuming the rate earned on the fund is one percentage point higher than today's rate during the whole savings period.
- Escenario economico desfavorable: possible retirement income assuming the rate earned on the fund is one percentage point lower than today's rate during the whole savings period.
- Pago unico: the insured may request the Retirement Coverage in a single payment in writing; under this option the institution pays the Reserve Fund balance in one payment.
- Renta mensual: the monthly rent is the Reserve Fund balance divided by the lifetime annuity factor registered before CNSF and valid at the retirement date.
