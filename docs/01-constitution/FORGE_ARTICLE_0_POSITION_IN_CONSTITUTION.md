## 1. Positioning Question

Is Article 0:

- a constitutional truth?
- a Skynet Law?
- a layer above Skynet?

## 2. Discovery Answer

Article 0 is best understood as a candidate layer above Skynet.

It should not initially be treated as an ordinary constitutional truth or one
Skynet Law among many.

Reason:

Article 0 defines the purpose of constitutional governance itself.

Skynet asks what intelligence systems must never do.

Article 0 explains why those prohibitions exist:

Forge must strengthen human judgment, not replace it.

## 4. Article 0 Role

Article 0 should answer:

- What is Forge ultimately protecting?
- What must success never destroy?
- What should remain true even if Forge becomes extraordinarily capable?
- What should govern the governor?

## 5. Constitution Role

The Constitution should define:

- identity
- domain boundaries

- approved truths
- amendment process

## 6. Skynet Role

Skynet should define:

- laws all intelligences obey
- non-negotiable constraints
- conflict behavior
- contextual policies
- governance boundaries

## 7. Intelligence Systems Role

Intelligence systems should:

- interpret facts
- generate insights
- recommend actions
- explain reasoning
- operate inside Skynet constraints
- support Article 0 through human judgment development

## 8. Why Article 0 Should Not Be a Normal Skynet Law

Skynet Laws are constraints.

Article 0 is a purpose-of-governance principle.

If Article 0 becomes one law among several, Forge may miss that it is the reason
the laws exist.

Example:

Evidence Before Judgment protects human reasoning.

Purpose Is Context protects human agency.

No Single Score protects human complexity.

Those are Skynet Laws candidates.

Article 0 is the parent reason:

Forge exists to strengthen human judgment, not replace it.

## 9. Why Article 0 Should Not Be Ratified Yet

Article 0 should not be ratified yet because:

- exact wording needs review
- relationship to Constitution v3 must be formalized
- relationship to Skynet must be reviewed
- governance owner is not defined
- practical dependency safeguards are not yet specified
- no amendment structure exists for Article 0

## 10. Possible Article 0 Wording

Candidate wording:

Forge exists to strengthen human judgment, not replace it.

Expanded wording:

Forge may augment, inform, teach and accelerate professional judgment, but must
never cultivate dependency, replace human responsibility or become the final
authority on human potential.

Not ratified.

## 11. Success Without Dependency

If Forge becomes extraordinarily successful, success should look like:

- advisors make better decisions
- advisors understand why decisions are better
- advisors become more capable over time
- advisors can explain recommendations
- managers coach better rather than command more
- users rely on Forge as a thinking partner, not final authority
- Forge transfers patterns of judgment, not just answers
- professional responsibility remains human

Failure would look like:

- users obey without understanding
- users lose confidence without Forge
- managers outsource judgment to Forge
- recommendations become commands
- professional development stalls because Forge does the thinking

## 12. Discovery Verdict

CANDIDATE FOR ARTICLE 0.

Article 0 appears to sit above Skynet as the purpose of governance.

No ratification occurs in this document.
