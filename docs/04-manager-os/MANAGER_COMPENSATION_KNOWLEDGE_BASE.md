## Core Principle

The PCV 2026 rewards construction of solid and sustainable teams. The manager role is not only production supervision; it is:

- Recruit.
- Develop.
- Retain.
- Promote.

Forge should treat compensation as a decision system around leadership behaviors and team quality, not only as a payout calculator.

## Compensation Philosophy

The plan is organized around three income pillars:

### Cimientos

Purpose:

- Give fixed support while the Partner builds the unit.

Representative compensation elements:

- Apoyos Fijos.
- Bono de Transicion.

### Construccion

Purpose:

- Reward productivity and daily activity generated by the team.

Representative compensation elements:

- Bono de Productividad.
- Bono de Produccion.
- Bono de Actividad.

### Expansion

Purpose:

- Reward growth, recruiting, development, retention, and promotion.

Representative compensation elements:

- Bono de Conexion.
- Bono de Desarrollo.
- Bono de Alta Partner.

## Global Access Rules

These rules affect access to variable compensation and should be modeled as shared eligibility gates, not duplicated inside each future bonus engine.

### Active Status

Rule:

- Estatus activo is indispensable for Partner and advisor.

Required data:

- Partner active status.
- Advisor active status.
- Status effective dates.
- Status source.

Risks:

- Treating active status as a static flag instead of time-based eligibility.
- Paying a bonus for a period in which the Partner or advisor was inactive.

### LIMRA

Rule:

- Minimum LIMRA is 78%.
- LIMRA is described as conservation of premiums and quality of initial sale.

Learning curve:

- Months 1-12: automatic base index.
- Months 13-24: greater of real or base.
- Month 25+: real calculated index.

Required data:

- Advisor tenure in months.
- Base LIMRA index.
- Real LIMRA index.
- Premium conservation data.
- Measurement period.

Risks:

- Hardcoding 78% without organization/rules versioning.
- Applying real LIMRA before the curve says it applies.
- Recalculating historical periods with newer rules.

### IGC

Rule:

- Minimum IGC is 86%.
- IGC is described as general conservation / long-term retention of cartera.

Learning curve:

- Months 1-12: automatic base index.
- Months 13-24: greater of real or base.
- Month 25+: real calculated index.

Required data:

- Advisor tenure in months.
- Base IGC index.
- Real IGC index.
- Portfolio conservation data.
- Measurement period.

Risks:

- Treating IGC and LIMRA as interchangeable.
- Calculating advisor qualification without the correct tenure curve.

## Key Classifications

### Asesor Calificado

Observed definition:

- Average monthly initial commissions greater than $9,000.
- Meets minimum LIMRA and IGC.

Compensation impact:

- Unlocks high-productivity payments.
- Counts toward productivity multiplier.
- Counts toward activity bonus only if minimum tenure is met.

Required data:

- Advisor initial commissions.
- Average monthly initial commissions.
- LIMRA.
- IGC.
- Advisor class / tenure segment.
- Advisor tenure in months.
- Measurement period.

Risks:

- Hardcoding $9,000.
- Using total commissions instead of initial commissions.
- Counting activity before the minimum tenure rule.

### Asesor No Calificado

Observed definition:

- Advisor has activity but does not reach commission target or required indexes.

Compensation impact:

- Generates Bono de Produccion with reduced payments.
- Does not unlock high-productivity treatment.

Required data:

- Activity evidence.
- Initial commission amount.
- LIMRA / IGC result.
- Qualification failure reason.

Risks:

- Treating no calificado as no activity.
- Excluding non-qualified advisors from all compensation when they may still generate production bonus.

## Bonus Map

### 1. Bono de Transicion

Purpose:

- Generate immediate cash flow while the Partner starts recruiting.

Observed period:

- Months 1-6.

Mechanic:

- Partner receives the equivalent of commissions generated by the cartera assigned to direct key.

Key condition:

- Does not require active administration or client intervention.
- It is transition income.

Variables:

- Partner start date.
- Partner direct key.
- Assigned cartera.
- Commissions generated by assigned cartera.
- Eligible month number from Partner start.

Metrics required:

- Monthly assigned cartera commissions.
- Months since Partner start.

Events required:

- Partner start / activation.
- Direct key assignment.
- Cartera assignment.
- Monthly commission generation event.

Dependencies:

- Assigned cartera source of truth.
- Commission source of truth.
- Effective dates for assignment.

Hardcode risks:

- Hardcoding months 1-6.
- Assuming assigned cartera always belongs to the same direct key.
- Treating transition income as manager-produced activity.

Possible future engine:

- `manager-transition-bonus-engine`

### 2. Bono de Productividad

Purpose:

- Reward earnings generated by qualified advisors.

Observed structure:

- Part 1: base payment by advisor commission range and class.
- Part 2: multiplier based on number of qualified advisors.

Eligibility:

- Advisor must be qualified for high-productivity treatment.
- Partner needs required indexes and active status.

Base percentage table observed:

| Average commissions | Clase CC | Clase 1C | Clase 2C | Clase 3C | Consolidados |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| $9,000 - $18,000 | 25.0% | 30.0% | 13.5% | 13.5% | 13.5% |
| $18,001 - $30,000 | 30.0% | 35.0% | 16.0% | 16.0% | 16.0% |
| $30,001 or more | 35.0% | 40.0% | 18.5% | 18.5% | 18.5% |

Class definitions observed:

- CC: first 12 months.
- 1C: months 13-24.
- 2C / 3C / Consolidated: shown as classes, but the PDF excerpt does not define exact tenure boundaries.

Multiplier table observed:

| Qualified advisors | Multiplier |
| ---: | ---: |
| 3 | 30% |
| 5 | 50% |
| 10 | 100% |

Formula described by the document:

- Bonus base + (Bonus base x multiplier percentage) = total.

Vital requirement:

- To collect 100%, the Partner needs 1 Training Allowance winner in the quarter.
- Without a TA winner, the bonus is paid only at 80%.

Variables:

- Advisor qualification status.
- Advisor class.
- Advisor average commissions.
- Applicable productivity percentage.
- Number of qualified advisors.
- Multiplier percentage.
- Quarter TA winner count.
- TA winner presence.
- Productivity payout reduction factor.

Metrics required:

- Qualified advisor count.
- Average monthly initial commissions by advisor.
- Advisor class distribution.
- TA winners by quarter.
- Productivity base amount.
- Productivity multiplier amount.

Events required:

- Advisor qualification event.
- Advisor class transition event.
- Commission close / commission posted event.
- TA winner event.
- Quarter close event.

Dependencies:

- LIMRA gate.
- IGC gate.
- Active status.
- Advisor tenure.
- Commission metric source.
- TA winner source.

Hardcode risks:

- Hardcoding $9,000, $18,000, $30,000, percentages, or multiplier tiers.
- Treating 2C / 3C / Consolidated as known without source rules.
- Assuming the 80% reduction is permanent across plan versions.
- Counting qualified advisors without considering time period.

Possible future engines:

- `manager-advisor-qualification-engine`
- `manager-productivity-base-engine`
- `manager-productivity-multiplier-engine`
- `manager-ta-dependency-engine`

| --- | ---: |
| Nueva Organizacion | 13.5% |

Variables:

- Organization type.
- Applicable production percentage.
- Unit LIMRA.
- Unit IGC.

Metrics required:

- Non-qualified active advisor count.

Events required:

### 4. Bono de Actividad

Purpose:

- Reward volume of policies from qualified advisors across Vida + GMM.

Observed scale:

| Number of policies | Percentage |
| ---: | ---: |
| 2 | 10% |
| 3 | 15% |
| 4 | 20% |
| 5 | 25% |
| 6 or more | 30% |

Important note:

- Only qualified advisors with minimum tenure of 3 months count.

Observed calculation pattern:

- Average policies from counted qualified advisors determines tier.
- Bonus applies to qualified commissions.

Variables:

- Policy count by advisor.
- Line of business: Vida / GMM.
- Advisor qualification status.
- Advisor tenure.
- Qualified commissions.
- Activity tier.
- Activity percentage.

Metrics required:

- Policy volume by advisor.
- Average policy count across eligible qualified advisors.
- Qualified advisor count.
- Qualified commission total.

Events required:

- Policy issued event.
- Policy counted for period event.
- Advisor reaches 3-month tenure event.

Dependencies:

- Advisor tenure.
- Advisor qualification.

Hardcode risks:

- Hardcoding 3-month minimum.
- Mixing Vida and GMM without source line-of-business tagging.
- Counting submitted policies instead of eligible issued policies if the source requires issuance.
- Applying tier to all production instead of qualified commissions.

Possible future engine:

- `manager-activity-bonus-engine`

Variables:

- Advisor activation / alta date.

Metrics required:

Events required:

Dependencies:

## Cross-Bonus Dependencies

### Advisor Qualification

Used by:

- Bono de Productividad.
- Bono de Produccion.
- Bono de Actividad.

Needed because:

- Qualified advisors unlock high-productivity rules.
- Non-qualified advisors may still generate production bonus.
- Activity bonus counts only qualified advisors with minimum tenure.

### LIMRA / IGC

Used by:

- Global access.
- Advisor qualification.
- Bono de Produccion.
- Productividad and other high compensation gates.

Needed because:

- PCV rewards quality and conservation, not only sales volume.

### Training Allowance

Used by:

- Bono de Productividad.
- Apoyos Fijos.

Needed because:

- One TA winner in the quarter is required to collect 100% productivity.
- Accumulated TA winners are required for fixed support.

### Advisor Tenure / Class

Used by:

- Advisor qualification.
- Productividad base table.
- Actividad minimum tenure.
- Conexion months 2-3.
- Desarrollo months 4-15.

Needed because:

- Different income rules apply at different advisor lifecycle stages.

### Partner Tenure

Used by:

- Bono de Transicion.
- Apoyos Fijos.
- Alta Partner payment schedule.

Needed because:

- Some bonuses are startup support and should expire or decline over time.

### Policy Volume

Used by:

- Bono de Actividad.
- Bono de Conexion.
- Bono de Desarrollo.

Needed because:

- PCV links manager income to advisor activity and development outcomes.
