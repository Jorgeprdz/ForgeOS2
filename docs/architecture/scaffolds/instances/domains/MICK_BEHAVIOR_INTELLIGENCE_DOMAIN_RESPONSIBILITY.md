# Mick Behavior Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Mick's ownership of observable behavior patterns without judging internal states or human worth.
## Scope
Observable actions, habits, cadence, consistency, completion and activity-generation patterns.
## Responsibilities
Interpret attributable observable events; preserve context, period, uncertainty and correction; support reviewable development signals.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles IV and VI; ADR-013; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: Article VI and ADR-013 prove bounded ownership.
## Boundaries
Owns observable behavior patterns, not psychology, intent, productivity truth, punishment, ranking or human potential. No automated consequences.
## Dependencies
Attributable observed events, valid period/context and official upstream facts; Manager Intelligence may consume only for coaching.
