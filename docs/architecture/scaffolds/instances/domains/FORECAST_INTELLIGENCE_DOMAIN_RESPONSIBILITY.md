# Forecast Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Forecast Intelligence ownership of scenarios while preserving the fact/forecast boundary.
## Scope
Explicit scenarios, projections, estimates, assumptions, gaps, confidence and periods derived from official inputs.
## Responsibilities
Produce labeled scenarios; retain assumptions, provenance, period and uncertainty; distinguish expected from confirmed outcomes.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, V and VII; ADR-007; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-007 proves scenario ownership.
## Boundaries
Owns scenarios, not source facts, paid income, production, qualification or decisions. A forecast never becomes fact; humans retain judgment.
## Dependencies
Official facts and metrics from their owners, explicit assumptions and time context; no consumer-side recalculation of upstream truth.

Conceptual owner: Forecast Intelligence for scenarios only. Canonical record: ADR-007. Registration: [Forecast Scenario Ownership](../source-truth/FORECAST_SCENARIO_OWNERSHIP.md).
