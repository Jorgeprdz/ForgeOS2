# Economic Motivation Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document ownership of responsible economic-motivation presentation without creating economic evidence or client pressure.
## Scope
Evidence-aware presentation of income goals, economic gaps, compensation scenarios and tradeoffs for advisor reflection.
## Responsibilities
Preserve source status, uncertainty and Client First; frame money as context, never pressure; suggest human review when evidence is insufficient.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, IV and VII; ADR-018; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-018 proves presentation ownership.
## Boundaries
Owns responsible presentation only; not Economic Evidence, compensation/forecast truth, NBA priority, product recommendation, manipulation or execution.
## Dependencies
Official Economic Evidence, Compensation, Forecast and Business Planning outputs with source, owner, period, confidence and limits.
