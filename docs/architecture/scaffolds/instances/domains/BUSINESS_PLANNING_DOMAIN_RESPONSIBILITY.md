# Business Planning Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Business Planning ownership of action paths without converting plans into mandates or execution.
## Scope
Goals, gaps, constraints, priorities, action paths and review loops derived from official inputs.
## Responsibilities
Translate governed evidence and metrics into reviewable action paths; preserve assumptions, alternatives and human choice.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III–V and VII; ADR-012; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-012 proves action-path ownership.
## Boundaries
Owns action paths, not upstream facts, metrics, human decisions or execution. A plan is not an order.
## Dependencies
Official metrics, evidence, forecasts and declared goals; consumers must retain provenance and human checkpoints.
