# Compensation Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document ownership of rule-based compensation interpretation without inventing amounts, rules or payment truth.
## Scope
Commissions, bonuses, contests, eligibility, advances, retroactives and qualification states interpreted from evidence, rules, periods and snapshots.
## Responsibilities
Differentiate paid, earned, estimated, projected, provisional and unknown; preserve rule, production basis, period, snapshot and confidence.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, V and VII; ADR-017; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-017 proves interpretation ownership.
## Boundaries
Owns compensation rule interpretation, not rules, production facts, payment ledger, Forecast Truth, client suitability or human worth. Client First and human authority remain controlling.
## Dependencies
Official compensation Rule Packs/schedules, Production Events, eligibility evidence, period and RuleSnapshot; paid claims require payment evidence.
