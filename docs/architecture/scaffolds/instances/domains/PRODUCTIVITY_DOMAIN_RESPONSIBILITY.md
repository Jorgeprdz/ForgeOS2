# Productivity Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Productivity ownership of bounded productivity metrics without duplicating behavior or economic truth.
## Scope
Official activity-to-output, throughput, conversion and productivity metrics within defined contexts and periods.
## Responsibilities
Define and preserve metric meaning, formula, inputs, period and uncertainty; publish one official metric per context.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-002, ADR-014 and ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-014.
## Boundaries
Owns productivity metrics only; not compensation, behavior, motivation, human worth or managerial consequences. Consumers may not recalculate official metrics.
## Dependencies
Valid activity/output facts, formulas, periods and snapshots; contextual interpretations remain consumers.
