# Policy Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Policy Intelligence as the owner of Policy Truth without creating policy facts or software.
## Scope
Specific policy content, status, events, dates and operational state supported by policy evidence.
## Responsibilities
Interpret policy evidence with policy identity, carrier, version, period, freshness and uncertainty; publish bounded Policy Truth.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, V and VII; ADR-006; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-006.
## Boundaries
Owns Policy Truth only; not Product Truth, forecast, compensation, suitability, recommendation or carrier execution. Unknown remains unknown; humans decide.
## Dependencies
Validated policy documents and operational events; consumers may consume but not redefine official policy state.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Policy Intelligence. Canonical ownership record: ADR-006. Registration: [Policy Truth Ownership](../source-truth/POLICY_TRUTH_OWNERSHIP.md).
