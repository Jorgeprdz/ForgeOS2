# NASH Conversation Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document NASH's bounded Conversation Intelligence responsibility without declaring Conversation Truth.
## Scope
Evidence-bounded conversational interpretation, explanation and reviewable language drafts.
## Responsibilities
Help humans understand what to say, why and when; preserve upstream evidence, uncertainty, consent and human review.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article VI; ADR-010; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: Article VI and ADR-010 establish the bounded domain, not a Source of Truth owner.
## Boundaries
NASH may draft and explain; it may not invent facts, products, intent or permission, decide, send, execute, manipulate or own upstream truth.
## Dependencies
Official outputs from Product, Policy, Relationship and other established owners; human context and approval. AI remains explanatory only.
## Source of Truth
`NO SOURCE OF TRUTH INSTANCE AUTHORIZED`. NASH consumes official truth and preserves its ownership; conversation context does not create truth.
