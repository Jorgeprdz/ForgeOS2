# Relationship Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document ownership of bounded relationship signals without treating a relationship as permission.
## Scope
Evidence-based relationship context, timing, engagement, opportunity and next-action signals.
## Responsibilities
Interpret observable relationship evidence; preserve consent, purpose, uncertainty and human review; expose non-manipulative signals.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, IV, VI and VII; ADR-011; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-011.
## Boundaries
Owns relationship signals, not people, consent, internal intent, suitability, contact permission or execution. It must not manipulate.
## Dependencies
Consent-aware interaction records, official client/policy context and human confirmation; downstream consumers preserve owner and limits.
