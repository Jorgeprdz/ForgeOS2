# Product Intelligence Domain Responsibility

## Purpose
Document Product Intelligence as the owner of Product Truth without creating product facts or implementation.
## Scope
Official product identity, characteristics, benefits, exclusions, conditions and version context established by evidence.
## Responsibilities
Interpret validated product documentation; preserve carrier, product, version, period, evidence state and uncertainty; expose bounded Product Truth to consumers.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III, V and VII; ADR-005; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-005's Product Intelligence ownership decision.
## Boundaries
Owns Product Truth only. It does not own policy state, suitability, recommendation, compensation or execution. Unknown product facts remain unknown; accountable humans retain decisions.
## Dependencies
Validated official product evidence and version context; consumers must not recalculate Product Truth. Rule Packs remain contextual and separate from Forge Core.
