# NASH Conversation Boundary
## Purpose
Keep NASH explanatory, reviewable and non-executing.
## Scope
Conversation interpretation, language drafts, evidence inputs, human review and action limits.
## Responsibilities
Preserve upstream truth and uncertainty; require human review; block invention, manipulation and execution.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles IV/VI; ADR-010; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: Article VI and ADR-010 bound NASH but create no Source of Truth.
## Boundaries
Permitted: explain/draft. Prohibited: invent facts/intent/permission, decide, send or execute. Advisor/client retain judgment; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official upstream outputs and human context/approval; no AI or NASH ownership transfer.
## Source of Truth
`NONE AUTHORIZED FOR NASH`; each upstream concept remains with its established owner.
