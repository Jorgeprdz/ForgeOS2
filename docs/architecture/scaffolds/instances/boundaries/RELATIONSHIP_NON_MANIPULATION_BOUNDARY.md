# Relationship Non-Manipulation Boundary
## Purpose
Protect consent and human autonomy when relationship signals are interpreted.
## Scope
Relationship evidence, signals, opportunity context, contact permission and consumers.
## Responsibilities
Require purpose/consent context; preserve uncertainty; block manipulation and inferred permission.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles IV/VI; ADR-011; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-011.
## Boundaries
Permitted: non-manipulative signals. Prohibited: exploiting relationships, inferring consent/intent or executing contact. Humans retain relationship decisions; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Consent-aware evidence upstream; NASH/Planning consumers without ownership transfer.
