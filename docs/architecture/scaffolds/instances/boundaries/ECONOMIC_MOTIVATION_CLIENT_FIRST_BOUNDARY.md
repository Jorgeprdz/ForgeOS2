# Economic Motivation Client First Boundary
## Purpose
Keep economic context subordinate to Client First and free of pressure or manipulation.
## Scope
Income goals, gaps, compensation scenarios, tradeoffs, wording and human review.
## Responsibilities
Preserve upstream evidence/uncertainty; show Client First limits; block shame, fear, promises and payout-first action.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III/IV; ADR-018; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-018.
## Boundaries
Permitted: responsible context presentation. Prohibited: economic evidence creation, pressure, mandate, product recommendation or execution. Advisor/client retain decisions; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official evidence/Compensation/Forecast/Planning outputs upstream; advisor reflection downstream without ownership transfer.
