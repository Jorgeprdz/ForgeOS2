# Policy Truth Boundary
## Purpose
Protect Policy Truth from stale, mixed or invented policy state.
## Scope
Policy identity, content, events, dates, status, evidence and consumers.
## Responsibilities
Require attributable evidence, freshness and context; HOLD missing/conflicting state.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III/V; ADR-006; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-006.
## Boundaries
Permitted: evidence-backed policy interpretation. Prohibited: product truth, carrier action, recommendation or invented status. Humans retain decisions; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official policy documents/events upstream; consumers downstream without truth redefinition.
## Source of Truth
Policy Intelligence; [Policy Truth Ownership](../source-truth/POLICY_TRUTH_OWNERSHIP.md); policy identity/version/period mandatory.
