# Compensation Evidence Boundary
## Purpose
Prevent invented compensation and preserve paid/earned/estimated/projected distinctions.
## Scope
Rules, schedules, Production Events, eligibility, periods, RuleSnapshots, evidence states and consumers.
## Responsibilities
Require rule/evidence/period/snapshot; label states; HOLD missing/conflicting evidence; prevent historical rule mixing.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-017; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-017.
## Boundaries
Permitted: rule-based interpretation. Prohibited: invented amounts/rules/payments, guarantees, payout-first recommendation or human worth. Humans retain decisions; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official rules and Production Events upstream; economic consumers downstream without payment or rule ownership transfer.
