# Productivity Metric Boundary
## Purpose
Protect one-owner productivity metrics from duplicate calculation and human-worth use.
## Scope
Metric definitions, formulas, inputs, periods, snapshots and consumers.
## Responsibilities
Require governed definitions; preserve lineage; HOLD conflicting/missing context; prevent consumer recalculation.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-002/014; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-014.
## Boundaries
Permitted: official productivity metrics. Prohibited: behavior/compensation truth, ranking, punishment or worth. Humans retain judgment; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official activity/output facts and formula/snapshot upstream; consumers downstream without redefinition.
