# Business Planning Plan-to-Action Boundary
## Purpose
Separate reviewable planning from human decision and execution.
## Scope
Goals, gaps, action paths, review checkpoints and execution handoff.
## Responsibilities
Preserve official inputs, alternatives and assumptions; require explicit human choice before action.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles IV/V; ADR-012; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-012.
## Boundaries
Permitted: propose action paths. Prohibited: mandates, parallel metrics, task creation or execution. Advisor/manager retain decisions; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official upstream evidence/metrics/scenarios; human review downstream; no reverse authority.
