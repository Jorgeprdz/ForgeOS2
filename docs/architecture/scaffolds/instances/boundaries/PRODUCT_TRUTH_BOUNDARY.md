# Product Truth Boundary
## Purpose
Protect Product Truth from invention, cross-product mixing and consumer redefinition.
## Scope
Product evidence, identity, carrier, version, period, claims and consumers.
## Responsibilities
Permit evidence-backed interpretation; require provenance/uncertainty; HOLD unsupported or conflicting product claims.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III/V; ADR-005; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-005.
## Boundaries
Permitted: bounded Product Truth. Prohibited: invented products/benefits, suitability, compensation or execution. Human decision retained by advisor/client; failure is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official product evidence upstream; Product/Policy/Forecast and other consumers downstream without reverse ownership.
## Source of Truth
Product Intelligence; [Product Truth Ownership](../source-truth/PRODUCT_TRUTH_OWNERSHIP.md); version/period mandatory.
