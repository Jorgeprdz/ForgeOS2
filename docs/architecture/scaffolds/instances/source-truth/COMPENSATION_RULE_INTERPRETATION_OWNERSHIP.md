# Compensation Rule Interpretation Ownership
## Purpose
Register Compensation Intelligence ownership of rule-based compensation interpretation.
## Scope
Commission, bonus, contest, eligibility, advance, retroactive and qualification interpretations by rule/period/snapshot.
## Responsibilities
Preserve evidence, rule, production basis, period, status and confidence; distinguish paid/earned/estimated/projected/unknown.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-017; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-017.
## Boundaries
No rule, production fact, payment ledger, guarantee or product recommendation is created. Missing evidence/rules yield `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official schedules/Rule Packs, Production Events, eligibility, RuleSnapshot and payment evidence when paid is claimed.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Compensation Intelligence for interpretation. Canonical definition: ADR-017. Rule, production and payment source owners remain separate.
