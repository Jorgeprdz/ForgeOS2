# Relationship Signal Ownership
## Purpose
Register Relationship Intelligence's ownership of bounded relationship signals.
## Scope
Evidence-based engagement, timing, opportunity and next-action context signals.
## Responsibilities
Preserve consent, purpose, source, uncertainty and correction; prevent manipulation or parallel relationship signal truth.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III/IV/VI; ADR-011; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-011.
## Boundaries
Signals do not own people, intent, consent or contact permission. Ambiguity is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED` and requires human review.
## Dependencies
Consent-aware interaction evidence and official client/policy context.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Relationship Intelligence. Canonical definition: ADR-011. Evidence-source owners remain unchanged.
