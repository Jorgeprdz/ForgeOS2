# Action Path Ownership
## Purpose
Register Business Planning's ownership of evidence-bounded action paths.
## Scope
Goals, gaps, constraints, priorities, alternatives, steps and review loops.
## Responsibilities
Retain upstream provenance and human choice; prevent plans becoming mandates or parallel metric truth.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Articles III–V; ADR-012; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-012.
## Boundaries
Action paths do not own inputs, decisions or execution. Missing authority/evidence is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official facts/metrics/scenarios and human-declared goals/constraints.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Business Planning for action paths. Canonical definition: ADR-012. Input owners remain authoritative.
