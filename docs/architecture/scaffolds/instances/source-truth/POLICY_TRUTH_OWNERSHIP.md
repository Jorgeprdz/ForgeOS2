# Policy Truth Ownership
## Purpose
Register Policy Intelligence's already-authorized ownership of Policy Truth.
## Scope
Specific policy content, state, dates and events within policy identity and period.
## Responsibilities
Preserve evidence provenance, freshness, context and conflict handling; prevent parallel policy state.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-006; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-006.
## Boundaries
Registration only; no policy event, product fact, recommendation or carrier authority is created. Missing/conflicting evidence is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Official policy documents and attributable operational events; evidence validity under ADR-001.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Policy Intelligence. Canonical definition: ADR-006. Source owner remains the identified policy issuer/system; version and period are mandatory.
