# Productivity Metric Ownership
## Purpose
Register Productivity's ownership of official productivity metrics.
## Scope
Activity-to-output, throughput, conversion and output metrics in defined contexts/periods.
## Responsibilities
Preserve definition, formula, inputs, snapshot and period; prevent duplicate official calculations.
## Authority
Unified Constitution Article III; ADR-002 and ADR-014; ADR-020; SG-002 Gate. Owner evidence: ADR-014.
## Boundaries
No behavior, compensation, motivation or human-worth ownership. Missing formula/context is `UNKNOWN / BLOCKED`.
## Dependencies
Valid activity/output facts, governed formula, period and snapshot.
## Source of Truth
Conceptual owner: Productivity. Canonical definition: ADR-014. Source-fact owners remain unchanged; consumers cannot recalculate official truth.
