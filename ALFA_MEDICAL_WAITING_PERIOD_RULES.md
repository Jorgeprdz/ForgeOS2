# Alfa Medical Waiting Period Rules

Status: DISCOVERY ONLY

## AM-WAIT-001

Description: Covered accidents are covered from day one.

Required Inputs:
- Policy start date.
- Accident date.
- Accident facts.
- Preexistence check.

Conditions:
- Event must meet accident definition.
- Event must not be excluded.

Exceptions:
- First expense after 30 days can cause event not to be treated as accident.
- Alcohol/drug, intentional, professional/racing or other exclusions may apply.

Unknowns:
- Whether first expense timing is documented.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, II Accidente; VI.2.

## AM-WAIT-002

Description: Certain acute illnesses are covered from day one if not
preexisting.

Required Inputs:
- Diagnosis.
- Policy start date.
- Preexistence evidence.

Conditions:
- Diagnosis must be one of listed day-one illnesses:
  appendicitis acute, food intoxication, infectious diarrhea/gastroenteritis,
  heat stroke, traumatic pulmonary thrombosis, traumatic cerebral hemorrhage,
  acute myocardial infarction without coronary disease.

Exceptions:
- Preexistence.
- Different diagnosis than listed.

Unknowns:
- Medical classification details.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VI.2.

## AM-WAIT-003

Description: Non-listed illnesses/lesions have 30-day waiting period.

Required Inputs:
- Policy start date.
- First symptom date.
- Diagnosis date.
- First expense date.

Conditions:
- Illness or lesion is not listed as day-one and not under special waiting
  period.
- Thirty uninterrupted days must pass from coverage start.

Exceptions:
- Event starts or manifests during the waiting period: not covered, including
  later expenses.

Unknowns:
- Whether manifestation date differs from diagnosis date.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VI.2.

## AM-WAIT-004

Description: Listed third-year conditions are covered from the start of the
third year of continuous coverage.

Required Inputs:
- Continuous coverage date.
- Diagnosis.
- First symptom/expense date.

Conditions:
- Condition must be in listed third-year group, including hemorrhoids,
  venous insufficiency, tonsils/adenoids, knee structures except bone fracture,
  nose/sinuses disease, certain spine diseases, toe phalanges joint diseases,
  gallbladder/biliary, urinary lithiasis, acid-peptic disease/GERD, mammary
  glands including breast cancer, and female reproductive system diseases
  including cancer.

Exceptions:
- Particular exclusions.
- Preexistence.
- Second valuation/programming requirements for some spine/hernias.

Unknowns:
- Whether symptoms existed before third year.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VI.1.

## AM-WAIT-005

Description: Congenital/genetic diseases for insureds born outside policy
vigency are covered from the third year if no prior evidence existed.

Required Inputs:
- Birth/vigency status.
- Coverage continuity.
- Prior diagnosis evidence.
- Prior expense evidence.

Conditions:
- Three years of continuous coverage.
- Before third year there must have been no diagnosis, no known/desapercibida
  disease evidence and no expenses.

Exceptions:
- Preexisting evidence before third year.

Unknowns:
- Meaning and proof of "desapercibida" in practice.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.4.

## AM-WAIT-006

Description: Hernias are covered from the third year under conditions.

Required Inputs:
- Hernia diagnosis.
- Coverage continuity.
- Prior diagnosis/symptom/expense evidence.
- Hernia type.

Conditions:
- Third year of continuous coverage.
- No prior diagnosis, awareness or expenses before third year.
- Disc pathologies/herniated discs require second valuation before treatment.

Exceptions:
- Diastasis of rectus abdominal muscles, including ventral hernia and/or
  abdominoplasty, except when hernia is consequence of a covered condition.

Unknowns:
- Clinical relation to covered condition.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.8.

## AM-WAIT-007

Description: Maternity requires 10 months of continuous coverage at birth.

Required Inputs:
- Mother's age.
- Coverage continuity.
- Birth date.
- Pregnancy/birth documentation.

Conditions:
- Mother must have at least 10 months of continuous coverage at birth.
- Age range and other maternity conditions apply.

Exceptions:
- Waiting-period elimination/reduction does not apply to maternity.

Unknowns:
- Whether prior SMNYL GMM individual/family policy continuity qualifies.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.1.

## AM-WAIT-008

Description: Fetal surgery requires 24 months of continuous coverage.

Required Inputs:
- Mother's coverage continuity.
- Gestational week.
- Procedure type.
- Medical file.

Conditions:
- Mother must have at least 24 months of continuous coverage at time of
  surgery.
- Additional fetal surgery rules apply.

Exceptions:
- Reproduction-assisted maternity.
- Non-listed procedures.

Unknowns:
- Whether recognized antiquity can apply; source says recognition of antiquity
  does not reduce this period.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.18; VI.3.

## AM-WAIT-009

Description: Bariatric surgery requires sixth year of continuous coverage.

Required Inputs:
- Coverage continuity.
- IMC.
- Comorbid diagnosis.
- Multidisciplinary protocol evidence.

Conditions:
- From the sixth year of continuous coverage.
- One time in insured lifetime.
- IMC and listed comorbidity requirements.

Exceptions:
- Complications and pre/post consultations are not covered.
- Recognition of antiquity does not apply.

Unknowns:
- Exact handling of prior bariatric procedures outside SMNYL.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, IV.13; VI.3.

## AM-WAIT-010

Description: Recognition of antiquity can reduce or eliminate only certain
waiting periods and is not retroactive coverage.

Required Inputs:
- Prior coverage evidence.
- Recognition in caratula.
- Condition type.

Conditions:
- Must be requested and recognized.
- Only affects certain waiting periods.

Exceptions:
- Does not eliminate/reduce maternity, congenital/genetic born-outside-vigency,
  hernias, bariatric surgery, fetal surgery or optional HIV/AIDS waiting rule.
- Does not cover preexisting diseases or past claims from another policy.

Unknowns:
- Required evidence for recognition.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VI.3.
