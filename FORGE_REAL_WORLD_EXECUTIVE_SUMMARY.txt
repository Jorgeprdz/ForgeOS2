FORGE REAL WORLD CASE REVIEW

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Forge reviewed:

- Alfa Medical Quote: cotizacion gmm.PDF
- Alfa Medical Policy Caratula: caratula poliza gmm.pdf

The most important finding:

The quote and policy caratula do not appear to belong to the same insured.

Quote insured:
Lariza Saenz, female, age 23.

Policy insured:
Franco Villanueva Najera, male, age 0.

This should be clarified before treating the quote as the proposal that became
the policy.

2. WHAT THE QUOTE PROPOSED

The quote proposed:

- Alfa Medical Integro
- National territoriality
- Gamma tabulator
- $170,000,000 sum insured
- $25,000 deductible
- 10% coinsurance
- $97,000 coinsurance cap
- Annual premium: $47,388.27
- Optional accident deductible elimination

3. WHAT THE POLICY ISSUED

The caratula issued:

- Alfa Medical Integro
- Policy GM0000737905(N)
- National territoriality
- Zone CDMX 1
- Gamma tabulator
- $160,000,000 sum insured
- $40,000 deductible
- 10% coinsurance
- $85,000 coinsurance cap
- Monthly payment form
- CEDA active: accident deductible elimination
- VIH not covered
- Protection Patrimonial not covered

4. WHAT STAYED THE SAME

- Product: Alfa Medical
- Plan: Integro
- Territoriality: National
- Tabulator: Gamma
- Coinsurance: 10%
- Deductible scheme: Unico
- Coinsurance scheme: Unico
- Accident deductible elimination appears selected/issued

5. WHAT CHANGED

- Insured
- Age
- Start date
- Zone
- Sum insured
- Deductible
- Coinsurance cap
- Premium/payment structure
- Protection Patrimonial expectation

6. CLIENT CLARITY

The client truly purchased national medical-expense protection for Franco with:

- a large coverage limit
- a $40,000 deductible
- 10% cost participation after deductible
- an $85,000 coinsurance cap
- accident deductible elimination under policy conditions

The client should not assume:

- the quote is the policy
- every listed quote benefit is active
- national coverage works abroad
- accident deductible elimination means zero cost
- the insurer pays every provider charge without limits

7. ADVISOR NEXT STEP

The advisor should verify:

- Was the Lariza quote attached by mistake?
- Is there a matching quote for Franco?
- Did the client understand and accept the $40,000 deductible?
- Did the client expect Protection Patrimonial or foreign benefits?
- Should the advisor create a one-page issued-policy summary?

8. FORGE VALUE CREATED

Forge identifies the expectation gap before it becomes a claim problem.

The real value is not only reading the documents.

The real value is showing the advisor and client:

- what was proposed
- what was issued
- what changed
- what should be clarified now
