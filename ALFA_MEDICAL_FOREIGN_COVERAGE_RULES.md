# Alfa Medical Foreign Coverage Rules

Status: DISCOVERY ONLY

## AM-FOR-001

Description: Basic coverage depends on the territory stated in the policy
caratula.

Required Inputs:
- Caratula territory.
- Location of care.
- Residence.

Conditions:
- Covered expenses must be incurred within stipulated territory unless a
  foreign optional/assistance benefit applies.

Exceptions:
- Optional foreign coverages.

Unknowns:
- Exact territory wording in caratula.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, I Objeto; II Residencia.

## AM-FOR-002

Description: Extension Abroad must be contracted and is tied to Catastrophic
Diseases Abroad.

Required Inputs:
- Caratula optional coverage.
- Catastrophic Diseases Abroad inclusion.

Conditions:
- Extension Abroad indemnifies covered accidents and illnesses abroad.
- Contracting is conditioned on inclusion of Catastrophic Diseases Abroad.

Exceptions:
- Coverage ceases after continuous foreign stay limit.

Unknowns:
- Real caratula representation.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.3.

## AM-FOR-003

Description: Programmed foreign attention under Extension Abroad has modified
deductible/coinsurance/cap rules.

Required Inputs:
- Extension Abroad contracted.
- Programmed-before-insurer evidence.
- Deductible.
- Coinsurance.

Conditions:
- Care abroad must be programmed before insurer.
- Deductible is modified per clause.
- Coinsurance has minimum.
- Foreign cap applies.

Exceptions:
- Non-programmed care uses different rules.

Unknowns:
- Operational meaning of "programmed."

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.3.A.

## AM-FOR-004

Description: Non-programmed foreign attention under Extension Abroad is paid by
reimbursement under more restrictive financial rules.

Required Inputs:
- Extension Abroad contracted.
- Non-programmed status.
- Receipts.
- Catalog/plan/zone.

Conditions:
- Payment through reimbursement.
- Deductible/coinsurance/cap rules differ from programmed care.
- Honorarium and hospital room/ICU limits apply.

Exceptions:
- Missing medical information access releases insurer obligation.

Unknowns:
- Exchange rate and reimbursement process.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.3.A.

## AM-FOR-005

Description: Catastrophic Diseases Abroad covers only listed catastrophic
diseases.

Required Inputs:
- Optional coverage status.
- Diagnosis.
- Disease category.

Conditions:
- Disease must fit listed categories: central nervous system diseases,
  vascular CNS events with permanent sequelae, cardiac surgery, cancer,
  listed transplants, major trauma treatment and chronic renal insufficiency.

Exceptions:
- Particular exclusions for this benefit.

Unknowns:
- Whether diagnosis qualifies as listed category.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.4.

## AM-FOR-006

Description: Programmed catastrophic care abroad requires prior diagnosis and
advance notice.

Required Inputs:
- Specialist diagnosis.
- Clinical studies.
- Programmed care date.
- Notice date.

Conditions:
- Diagnosis must be supported by clinical studies.
- Must be obtained at least 10 business days before treatment start for
  programmed treatment.

Exceptions:
- Emergency or non-programmed pathway uses different rules.

Unknowns:
- Emergency pathway evidence standard.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.4.

## AM-FOR-007

Description: Non-programmed catastrophic care abroad uses separate deductible,
coinsurance and no-cap rules.

Required Inputs:
- Catastrophic coverage.
- Non-programmed status.
- Deductible.
- Coinsurance.
- Extension Abroad status.

Conditions:
- Deductible remains contracted.
- Coinsurance is modified.
- No coinsurance cap.
- If no Extension Abroad, honorarium catalog may be adjusted per clause.

Exceptions:
- Missing access to medical information releases insurer obligation.

Unknowns:
- Exact interaction with Extension Abroad in real policy.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.4.

## AM-FOR-008

Description: Catastrophic Diseases Abroad excludes specific diseases and
expenses.

Required Inputs:
- Diagnosis.
- Donor/compatibility expenses.
- Care type.

Conditions:
- Cervical cancer in situ and skin cancer except malignant melanoma are not
  catastrophic diseases.
- Certain congenital/genetic CNS diseases, transient cerebral ischemia,
  senility/deterioration care and rejected donor/compatibility expenses are
  excluded.

Exceptions:
- Listed CNS exceptions.

Unknowns:
- Disease staging and classification.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.4 exclusions.

## AM-FOR-009

Description: Students and Temporary Workers Abroad covers urgencies under
specific evidence and duration rules.

Required Inputs:
- Optional coverage status.
- Student/temporary worker proof.
- Urgency evidence.
- Foreign stay duration.

Conditions:
- Insured must previously prove foreign stay as student or temporary worker.
- Maximum continuous stay rule applies.
- Event must meet medical urgency definition.
- Coverage has separate sum insured and deductible in caratula.

Exceptions:
- Maternity, preexisting events, events initially occurring in Mexico and
  foreign rehabilitation are excluded.

Unknowns:
- Documents accepted to prove student/temporary worker status.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.6.

## AM-FOR-010

Description: Assistance Abroad covers foreign medical urgency through a
specific assistance benefit.

Required Inputs:
- Optional Assistance Abroad coverage.
- Urgency evidence.
- Assistance channel.
- Caratula values.

Conditions:
- Event must meet urgency definition.
- Deductible specified in caratula applies.
- No coinsurance applies under the stated urgency benefit.

Exceptions:
- Benefit-specific exclusions.

Unknowns:
- Channel and service provider process.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.9.

## AM-FOR-011

Description: Continuous stay abroad can terminate foreign benefit effects.

Required Inputs:
- Date left Mexico.
- Continuous foreign stay duration.
- Benefit type.
- Student/temporary worker evidence if applicable.

Conditions:
- Certain foreign benefits cease after stated continuous stay limits.
- National coverage may continue subject to policy.

Exceptions:
- Student/temporary worker benefit may continue if status continues and is
  notified/evidenced.

Unknowns:
- Exact travel evidence and clock rules.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, II Residencia; VIII.3; VIII.4; VIII.6.

## AM-FOR-012

Description: Foreign coverage requires access to medical information.

Required Inputs:
- Medical information request.
- Insured authorization/cooperation.

Conditions:
- Insured must allow insurer access to necessary medical information for claim
  review/payment.

Exceptions:
- If not met, insurer can be released from obligation.

Unknowns:
- Operational consent process.

Source:
- Alfa Medical Nuevos, VIII.3; VIII.4; VIII.6.
