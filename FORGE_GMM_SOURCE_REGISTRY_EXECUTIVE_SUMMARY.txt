7. FINAL QUESTION RESULT

If:

- Alfa Medical Manual 2025 says X
- Alfa Medical Conditions 2026 say Y
- Client Caratula says Z

Forge should:

1. Confirm product and policy.
2. Treat caratula as policy-specific authority.
3. Treat 2026 conditions as active product legal authority if applicable to
   policy/event date.
4. Treat 2025 manual as lower-authority reference or historical unless proven
   active for that policy period.
5. Use Z for policy-specific selection/values.
6. Use Y for product mechanics and limits.
7. Downgrade or ignore X if it contradicts Y.
8. Trigger human review if Z and Y materially conflict or effective dates are
   unclear.

Confidence:

- High if caratula and active conditions align.
- Medium if only manual conflict exists and manual is lower authority.
- Human Review Required if caratula and active conditions conflict.
